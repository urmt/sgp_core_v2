### Interpretations

| Claim | Evidence For | Evidence Against | Confidence |

| **Multiverse explains fine-tuning** | Garriga-Vilenkin (2006) predicted Λ near anthropic bound. Srednicki-Hartle (2010) causal-patch measure produces Λ distribution peaked near observed value.
