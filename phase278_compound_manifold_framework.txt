# Simultaneous Recursive Stressors and Manifold Stability

================================================================================
SECTION 1: FRAMEWORK DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

DEFINITION 1: Compound Stress
------------------------------
Compound stress applies multiple perturbations simultaneously:
- Recursion depth progression
- Heterogeneity (k_std = 0.4)
- Delay (tau = 2)

This tests whether manifold convergence is robust or artifact.

DEFINITION 2: Irreversible Fracture
-----------------------------------
A topology exhibits irreversible fracture when:
- localization > 0.05 (fracture threshold)
- AND recovery_delta >= 0.01 (no return to baseline)

If recovery_delta < 0.01, fracture is reversible.

DEFINITION 3: Manifold Convergence Survival
------------------------------------------
Manifold convergence survives if:
- All topologies remain bounded under compound stress
- Variance does not diverge
- Rollback remains exact

DEFINITION 4: Topology Reseparation
------------------------------------
Topology separation reappears if:
- compound_variance > recursion_only_variance
- Individual topologies diverge from universal band
- Localization spreads across wider range

DEFINITION 5: Delayed Rollback Under Stress
------------------------------------------
Rollback under compound stress tests:
- Forward: depths [0.0, 0.5, 1.0, 2.0]
- Backward: depths [1.5, 1.0, 0.0]
- Criterion: recovery_delta < 0.01

================================================================================
SECTION 2: CONDITIONS TESTED
================================================================================

CONDITION A: Recursion only
  - Baseline recursive depth progression
  - k = 0.5 constant
  - No additional perturbations

CONDITION B: Recursion + Delay
  - Recursion with temporal delay (tau=2)
  - Tests temporal perturbation stability

CONDITION C: Recursion + Heterogeneity
  - Recursion with heterogeneous coupling
  - k varies by ±0.4 around base
  - Tests coupling variance stability

CONDITION D: Compound (Recursion + Delay + Heterogeneity)
  - All perturbations combined
  - Tests true stress resistance

CONDITION E: Rollback under compound stress
  - Forward to depth 2.0
  - Backward to depth 0.0
  - Tests recovery under compound

================================================================================
SECTION 3: VERDICT CRITERIA
================================================================================

VERDICT: UNIVERSAL_MANIFOLD_PRESERVED
  - All Q1-Q5 PASS
  - Bounded localization maintained
  - Convergence survives
  - No separation
  - Exact rollback

VERDICT: COMPOUND_STRESS_RESEPARATION
  - Q3 FAIL (topology separation reappears)
  - Compound variance > baseline variance
  - Convergence breaks under stress

VERDICT: IRREVERSIBLE_FRACTURE
  - Q5 FAIL (irreversible fracture observed)
  - localization > 0.05 for some topology
  - AND recovery_delta >= 0.01

VERDICT: INCONCLUSIVE
  - Mixed results
  - Cannot determine convergence status
