## The Case

### Premise 1: Λ is anthropically bounded

**Evidence:** Four independent calculations confirm an upper bound
on Λ for galaxy formation:

- Weinberg (1987): Λ < ~10⁻¹¹⁸ (analytic perturbation theory)
- Tegmark et al. (2006): Λ < ~10⁻¹²⁰ (N-body simulation)
- Barnes (2012): Λ < ~10⁻¹¹⁶ (analytic review)
- Piran & Jimenez (2023): Λ bound within factor ~100 (N-body)

**Status: HIGH confidence.** The existence of an upper bound is
uncontroversial. The exact value varies by ~10⁴×, but the bound's
existence is confirmed across four independent methods.

**Relevance:** Observed Λ = 10⁻¹²² is ~10⁴× below the most
conservative bound. Anthropic selection explains this naturally.

---

### Premise 2: Carbon is the only chemically viable substrate for complex life

**Evidence:** Petkowski, Bains & Seager (2020) *Life* 10, 84 —
comprehensive review of organosilicon chemistry:

| Solvent | Silicon as building block? |
|---------|--------------------------|
| Water | **NO** — silica formation removes Si from solution |
| Cryosolvents | **NO** — all molecules insoluble |
| Sulfuric acid | **NO** — Si only as rare heteroatom |

**Status: MODERATE confidence.** The same lead author who proposed
silicon life (Bains 2004) now co-authors its refutation (2020).
No alternative biochemistry has published chemical feasibility.

**Implication:** The α/αs bounds for carbon production are relevant
for life in general. The "carbon chauvinism" objection is
scientifically weaker than the T500 analysis assumed.

### Premise 3: αs is fine-tuned at the ~±1.5% level for carbon production

**Evidence chain:**
- The Hoyle state in ¹²C (7.654 MeV above ground) is a resonance
  that enables the triple-alpha process
- Huang, Adams & Grohs (2019) *Astropart. Phys.* 105, 13: MESA
  stellar evolution models find ΔE_R ∈ [-300, +500] keV viable
  for carbon production
- Lähde, Meißner & Epelbaum (2020) *Eur. Phys. J. A* 56, 89:
  NLEFT calculation maps ΔE_R to Δαs/αs ≈ ±1.5%
- This means the strong coupling must be within ~±1.5% of its
  observed value for the universe to produce carbon

**Status: MODERATE confidence for the bound width (MESA+EFT).**
LOW confidence for the exact value (not independently replicated).

| Source | Bound | Replication |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Livio (1989) | ±0.5% | NOT replicated |
| Oberhummer (2000) | ±0.5% | Not independent |
| Huang (2019) + Lähde (2020) | ~±1.5% | MESA is open-source; NLEFT is independently testable |

The Huang+Lähde bound is wider (~±1.5%) but rests on modern,
potentially reproducible codes.

### Premise 4: α is also fine-tuned for carbon production

**Evidence:**
- Same triple-alpha process is sensitive to the Coulomb barrier
- Lähde et al. (2020): δα_em/α_em ≈ ±1.5% for carbon production
- The fine-structure constant affects both the Hoyle state energy
  and the Coulomb barrier for α capture

**Status: LOW-MODERATE confidence.** Less well-determined than αs.

---

### Premise 5: μ is bounded at the factor ~100 level for chemistry

**Evidence:**
- Carr & Rees (1979): μ < ~10⁻² (electron capture), μ > ~10⁻⁵
  (proton decay) — factor ~1000
- Adams (2019): μ ∈ [5.45×10⁻⁵, 0.00545] — factor ~100
- m_p > m_e: fundamental QED requirement for stable atoms

**Status: HIGH confidence for m_p > m_e. LOW confidence for
the factor ~100 bound (estimate, not simulation).**

---

### Premise 6: Four constants must simultaneously be life-permitting

**Effective degrees of freedom (conservative baseline):**
- Λ (independent — no known correlation with other constants)
- α (partially correlated with αs in GUTs, but not necessarily)
- αs (partially correlated with α)
- μ (independent — Yukawa couplings)

**Conservative estimate: 3–4 independent DoF.**

---

### Premise 7: No probability can be assigned, but the pattern is striking

**Honest statement:**

"We cannot compute a probability for this coincidence. No valid
measure exists. However, four fundamental constants (Λ, α, αs, μ)
all fall within ranges that allow complex carbon-based life.
For at least one constant (Λ), we have a complete anthropic
explanation. For the others, no non-anthropic explanation exists.
This does not prove design or a multiverse, but it is a genuine
scientific observation requiring explanation."
