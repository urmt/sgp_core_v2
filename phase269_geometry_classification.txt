A. LIKELY UNIVERSAL STRUCTURE
==============================

Properties that persist across ALL topologies tested:

   - Universal threshold: k_s ≈ 0.6
   - Confidence: HIGH
   - Evidence: All 5 topologies exhibit saturation

2. AMPLIFICATION DIRECTION
   - All topologies show transfer increase toward zero with k
   - dT/dk > 0 for k < saturation boundary
   - Confidence: HIGH
   - Evidence: Monotonic increase in all cases

3. BOUNDARY REGION LOCATION
   - All topologies have transition at k ≈ 0.6-0.7
   - Boundary between amplification and saturation
   - Confidence: MODERATE
   - Evidence: Similar onset across topologies
