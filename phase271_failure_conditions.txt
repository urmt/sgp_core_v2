SECTION 1: EXPLICIT FAIL CONDITIONS
================================================================================

The Phase 271 competitive geometry analysis FAILS if ANY of the
following conditions are met:

FAIL CONDITION 1: Propagation Geometry Randomizes
--------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Transfer values show no systematic k-dependence under competition
Evidence required: |dT/dk| < 0.01 for k ∈ [0, 1]
Current status: Clear k-dependence in dual topology → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 2: Competition Destroys All Topology Classes
-------------------------------------------------------------
Criterion: All topology classes become indistinguishable under competition
Evidence required: |T_A - T_B| < ε for all A, B pairs
Current status: AA (-0.009) vs ER (-0.033) remain distinguishable → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 3: Saturation Unstable Under Competition
-------------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Saturation boundary shifts non-systematically or disappears
Evidence required: k_s varies by >0.3 across competition conditions
Current status: k_s shifts from 0.6 to 0.4 (0.2 shift, systematic) → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 4: Localization Collapses Inconsistently
------------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Localization appears/disappears randomly with alpha
Evidence required: Non-monotonic |ΔP| with respect to alpha
Current status: |ΔP| decreases with alpha until alpha=1.0 → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 5: Coexistence Irreproducible
-------------------------------------------
Criterion: Dual topology manifold not reproducible across seeds
Evidence required: Var(T_dual) across seeds > 0.01
Current status: Variance = 0.000000 → NOT FAILING
