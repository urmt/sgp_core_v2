# RD-COVERAGE WARNING

**Source:** Research Director (RD-WELL.8A.R1 authorization)
**Status:** ACTIVE WARNING
**Date:** 2026-06-17

## Statement

**"Apparently precise cross-domain comparisons may arise from uneven replication coverage rather than genuine differences."**

This is now one of the largest methodological risks in the program.

## Evidence

Current effective replication:

| Domain | N |
|--------|---|
| MHD | 4 |
| RT | 2 |
| RB | 1 |
| AM | 1 |
| GS | 1 |

Single-instance estimates (N=1) cannot provide uncertainty estimates. Cross-domain comparisons dominated by single-instance estimates may appear more precise than they are.

## Implications

1. **False precision:** Single measurements appear precise but have no uncertainty.
2. **False differences:** Differences between domains may reflect sampling noise rather than genuine differences.
3. **False rankings:** Rankings based on single measurements are unstable.

## Required Protocol

1. **Minimum N=5** for any domain to be included in cross-domain comparisons.
2. **Report uncertainty** for all domains.
3. **Test whether differences exceed uncertainty.**

## Relationship to Other Warnings

- **RD-CI RULE:** Confidence intervals mandatory.
- **RD-SMALL-N WARNING:** Few domains may generate attractive but unstable associations.
- **RD-SURVIVOR BIAS WARNING:** Domains with more audits may have better coverage.
