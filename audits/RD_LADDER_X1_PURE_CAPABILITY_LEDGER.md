## Observation

**CPR = 1.00 at every transition.**

All lower-level capabilities are preserved at every rung. No capabilities are removed.

**CER decreases as levels increase.**

Early transitions add proportionally more new capabilities. Later transitions add proportionally fewer.

**The ladder is cumulative, not replacement-based.**

Every rung includes everything below it plus new capabilities.
