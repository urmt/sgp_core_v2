# RD-STABILIZATION WARNING

**Status:** ACTIVE WARNING
**Created:** 2026-06-19
**Trigger:** P10 revealed fragility in temporal signatures

## Warning

Apparent convergence under increasing replication depth may reflect averaging-induced smoothing rather than emergence of a genuine organizational property.

## Implication

Stabilization curves can falsely appear meaningful simply due to variance compression. P11 must distinguish:

1. True convergence
2. Slow drift
3. Averaging artifacts
4. Unstable oscillation

## Required Safeguards

For each stabilization curve, P11 computes:
- slope(N)
- second derivative
- convergence half-life
- plateau uncertainty

Do not rely only on visual stabilization.
