1. Derive the candidate independently from both chains

### 1. All Four Candidates Are Genuine Junctions

Each of the four candidates independently generates from both the physical chain and the logical chain, and resists further reduction.

They are not the same transformation expressed in different words. Each captures something different:
- **Recursion** is about self-reference
- **Constraint** is about bounding
- **Preservation** is about invariance
- **Distinction** is about identity creation

The four junctions may work together:

### 4. The Program Has Found Its Junctions

The junction search is complete. The program has identified four transformations that appear independently from both ends of the compositional ladder and resist further reduction.
