================================================================================
SECTION 1: FRAMEWORK DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

DEFINITION 1: Universal Manifold Stability
-------------------------------------------
The universal manifold remains stable under perturbation when
localization values remain bounded across all recursion conditions.

Stability criterion:
  max(localization) < 0.05

DEFINITION 2: Stochastic Recursion Ordering
-------------------------------------------
Stochastic ordering introduces random permutation of recursion
depths, testing whether attractor bands depend on order.

Order permutation:
  order = rng.permutation(int(depth) + 1)

DEFINITION 3: Delayed Rollback
------------------------------
Delayed rollback tests recovery after forward progression to
depth 2.0, followed by delayed backward traversal to depth 0.0.

Rollback sequence:
  Forward: depths [0.0, 0.5, 1.0, 2.0]
  Backward: [1.5, 1.0, 0.0] (2 cycles)

DEFINITION 4: Heterogeneous Coupling
------------------------------------
Heterogeneous coupling varies coupling coefficient k across
[0.3, 0.5, 0.7], testing convergence under varying signal mixing.

Recovery metric:
  recovery_delta = |backward_loc[final] - forward_loc[initial]|

DEFINITION 5: Topology Ordering Invariance
------------------------------------------
Expected fracture ordering:
  ER < AA < SW

Where ER (Erdos-Renyi) fractures before AA (all-to-all),
and AA fractures before SW (small-world).
