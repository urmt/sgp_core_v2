    but does NOT destabilize structure. Localization preserved.

Q3: Do propagation delays alter saturation boundaries?
A3: NO - Saturation boundary remains at k ≈ 0.6 regardless of delay.
    Delays do not shift boundaries.

Q4: Do heterogeneous systems preserve topology classes?

    |ΔP| difference < 0.02 between homogeneous and heterogeneous.

Q5: Does propagation stability survive interaction variability?
A5: YES - Variance across seeds = 0.000000. Extremely stable.
    Propagation geometry robust under all tested heterogeneity.
