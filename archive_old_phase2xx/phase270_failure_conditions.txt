================================================================================
SECTION 1: EXPLICIT FAIL CONDITIONS
================================================================================

The Phase 270 heterogeneous geometry analysis FAILS if ANY of the
following conditions are met:

FAIL CONDITION 1: Topology Classes Collapse Randomly
---------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Heterogeneity causes topology classes to become indistinguishable
Evidence required: |T(topo_A, het) - T(topo_B, het)| < ε for all A, B
Current status: Classes remain distinguishable → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 2: Saturation Boundaries Unstable
-------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Saturation boundary shifts significantly under heterogeneity
Evidence required: |k_s(het) - k_s(hom)| > 0.2
Current status: k_s ≈ 0.6 invariant → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 3: Localization Disappears Inconsistently
----------------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Localization varies randomly with heterogeneity level
Evidence required: |T(k=1, het_high) - T(k=1, het_low)| > 0.05
Current status: |T| bounded at all levels → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 4: Heterogeneity Destroys Propagation Structure
---------------------------------------------------------------
Criterion: Propagation geometry fundamentally changes with heterogeneity
Evidence required: Direction of dT/dk reverses
Current status: dT/dk positive throughout → NOT FAILING

FAIL CONDITION 5: Stability Non-Reproducible
---------------------------------------------
Criterion: Propagation stability varies across seeds
Evidence required: Var(T across seeds) > 0.01
Current status: Variance ≈ 0.000000 → NOT FAILING
