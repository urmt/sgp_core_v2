DIAGRAM 1: EMERGENCE PROGRESSION CHART
============================================================

Type: Multi-panel line chart
Data: 7 sectors × emergence E(d) vs depth d (log scale)
X-axis: Recursion depth d (1 to 10^8, log scale)
Y-axis: Emergence measure E(d) [0, 1]
Series: 7 sectors (P-A-N, P-A, Projection, P-N, Antisymmetry,
         Neutral, A-N) in distinct colors
Key feature: All curves decay with γ(d) form, maintain ordering
Annotation: γ(d) = 1/(1 + 1.5e-8(d-1)) overlay
Source: Phase 391-399

Visual output: 7 descending curves, never crossing, converging
toward zero at extreme depth. P-A-N always highest, A-N always lowest.

============================================================

Type: Bar chart with error bars
X-axis: 6 synthetic system types

      red = significant negative, gray = not significant)

            "Evolutionary systems show significant NEGATIVE correspondence"
