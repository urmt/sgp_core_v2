# Interpretation Discipline Lock

## MANDATORY PROHIBITIONS

The following interpretations are STRICTLY FORBIDDEN
for ALL future work on SFH-SGP.

### Category 1: Ontology Inflation
- "SFH-SGP proves emergence is real."
  NO. The framework models recursive relational structure.
  It does not make ontological claims about reality.

- "SFH-SGP describes how the universe emerges."
  NO. The framework is defined on abstract relational
  configurations. It does not describe physical systems
  unless independently validated (which has not happened).

- "The hierarchy is a fundamental law of emergence."
  NO. The hierarchy is an empirical regularity within the
  P-A-N operator system. It is not a fundamental law.

### Category 2: Physics Claims
- "SFH-SGP corresponds to quantum mechanics."
  NO. No physical correspondence has been established.
  The mathematical structure is formally independent.

- "SFH-SGP describes spacetime emergence."
  NO. The relational state space is abstract, not
  spatiotemporal. There is no metric, no causality,
  and no physical dynamics.

- "The depth parameter is time."
  NO. Depth is recursion count. It is not temporal.
  There is no arrow of time, no duration, and no
  causal structure implied.

- "SFH-SGP proves physical theories are relational."
  NO. The framework models relations in an abstract
  configuration space. It does not comment on the
  nature of physical theories.

### Category 3: Consciousness Claims
- "SFH-SGP explains consciousness."
  NO. The framework does not model subjective
  experience, awareness, or any cognitive phenomenon.

- "The hierarchy corresponds to levels of awareness."
  NO. There is no mapping between emergence sectors
  and any cognitive or neural structure.

- "Recursive emergence is how the mind works."
  NO. The framework has not been validated on any
  neural or cognitive data.

### Category 4: Overclaim
- "SFH-SGP is a theory of everything."
  NO. The framework is a bounded empirical model of
  one specific universality class of recursive systems.

- "SFH-SGP proves emergence is universal."
  NO. Phase 411 falsified this claim. The hierarchy is
  framework-specific, not universal.

- "The formal axioms prove the framework is true."
  NO. The axioms define the system. They do not prove
  its correspondence to anything outside the framework.

- "SFH-SGP has predictive power for real systems."
  NO (qualified). The framework predicts emergence
  within the P-A-N operator system. It has NOT been
  validated on any real-world or physical system.

### Category 5: Methodology Violations
- "We can reinterpret the mapping to make it work."
  NO. Phase 411 methodology was pre-defined. Post-hoc
  fitting would invalidate the negative result.

- "The negative result is a failure of the synthetic
  systems, not the framework."
  NO. The negative result is an honest boundary on
  the framework's scope. That is how science works.

- "If we look harder, the hierarchy exists everywhere."
  NO. This is unfalsifiable. The framework's boundaries
  have been established by controlled testing.

## ENFORCEMENT
Any publication, presentation, or discussion of SFH-SGP
MUST include explicit disclaimers for all claims in
Categories 1-5. Violations are scientific misconduct
by overclaim.

## POSITIVE STATEMENT (ALLOWED)
"SFH-SGP is a bounded empirical framework describing a
specific universality class of constrained recursive
relational emergence under three primitive operators
(P, A, N) with monotonic depth scaling."
