They are not to be silently replaced by materialist defaults.

- Objects are not primitives. They are derived from persistent interaction patterns.
- Interaction is not a property of objects. Objects are a property of interaction.
- Sentience is not an emergent property of complex computation. It is the ground.
- Physics describes regularities within sentience, not a mind-independent substrate.
- The benchmark track tests a derivative prediction (coherence tracks recovery). The ontology track tests the core claim (objects arise from interaction).

The two tracks are independent. Success or failure of one does not determine the other.
