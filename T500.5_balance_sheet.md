## Verdict

**The evidence supports one robust claim: the cosmological constant
must be smaller than ~10⁻¹¹⁶ for structure to form. This is observer
selection, not fine-tuning.**

**The evidence does NOT support:**
- That the universe is fine-tuned to one part in 10¹²⁰
- That the fine-structure constant is fine-tuned for life
- That the strong coupling constant is fine-tuned for life
- That the weak scale is fine-tuned for life
- That any joint probability can be meaningfully computed

**The popular narrative of cosmic fine-tuning is a rhetorical
construction, not a physics result.**
