**Date:** 2026-06  
**Status:** APPROVED (RD-CALIBRATION.1)

---

## Starting Point

The project assumes:

**Experience exists.**

Experience is self-evident first-person reality.

The project does NOT attempt to prove experience.

---

## The Actual Research Question

The project investigates:

> "What structures maximize coherent, fertile experience through interaction?"

This differs from:

> "How does experience emerge from matter?"

Those are separate research programs.

---

## Working Definitions (UNDER TEST)

**Observer:**  
Anything capable of interaction is an observer at the instant of interaction.

**Observation:**  
The sensing that drives interaction.

**Interaction:**  
Observation and interaction are two descriptions of the same event.

**Primitive Experience:**  
The intrinsic side of interaction.

**Consciousness:**  
Sentience being sentient.

**Reflection:**  
Experience modeling experience.

---

## Important Warning

These are working definitions.

No ontological claims are established.

All definitions are under test and may be revised or abandoned.
