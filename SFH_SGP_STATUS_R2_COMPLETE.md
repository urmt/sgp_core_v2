**Collapse forecasting (R2.1-A): PARTIAL.** Flow predicts collapse (r = -0.778, p = 0.001) but PCA (R² = 0.250) beats Φ-space RF (R² = 0.218) at predicting collapse slopes.

### T027 — Flow Invariant (VALID BUT FRAGILE)
Flow predicts collapse WITHOUT clusters (r = -0.778, p = 0.001). Flow beats the cluster model as a collapse predictor (flow R² = -0.257 vs cluster R² = -0.383). But the flow field fails under SpectralEmbedding, t-SNE, and synthetic system injection — it is real but not universally embedding-invariant.

### T028 — Transition Manifold Dynamics (PARTIALLY DYNAMICAL)
The bridge acts as a transit corridor in geodesic analysis. Null geometry destruction breaks prediction (Δ = 0.797). The stability tensor (4 × 17 Jacobian ∂Φ/∂ε) shows bridge systems have elevated m2_contribution sensitivity on the A-axis (6.77, 6.05 vs 2.16–4.47). Flow decorrelates immediately under perturbation (persistence σ ≈ 0.1).

### T029 — Geodesic Embedding (ENRICHES MANIFOLD)
Combining Φ-space distance with stability tensor Frobenius distance improves kNN flow prediction (r = 0.793 vs r = 0.434). Tensor-flow correlation is highly significant (z = 3.589). However, bridge betweenness remains low in the combined graph — the stability tensor captures genuine dynamical information but does not elevate bridge centrality.

## The Surviving Physics

The project started seeking **universal categories** across dynamical systems. That has been repeatedly falsified (P6 scaling law, cross-domain transfer failure). What survives is:

1. **Φ-space is real** — 4 emergence axes from 17 complexity measures, ~2 effective DOF
2. **Flow field on Φ-space is causal** — neighborhood asymmetry predicts collapse; destroying geometry destroys prediction
3. **Bridge is a dynamical transition zone** — not a category, not static geometry; a region of high flow between coherent and unstable regimes
4. **Stability tensor encodes perturbation response** — C/F axes are linear composites (constant derivative), A/R are nonlinear (system-dependent); bridge systems are m2_contribution-sensitive
5. **Combined Φ-stability metric improves prediction** — but the effect is modest (r = 0.161 vs 0.048) and explained primarily by flow prediction (r = 0.793 vs 0.434 via kNN)

**The core claim**: Reality organizes through dynamical transition regions between coherence and instability. This does not require universality, fixed categories, or ontology — only position in a flow field generated by the competition between coherent structure and fluctuating dynamics.
