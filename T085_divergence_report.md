# T085: Mapping–Generation Divergence Audit

## Question
Where exactly do T080's conceptual mapping and T081/T082's operational generation disagree about substrate emergence from meta-constraints?

## Design
Reconstructed T080 as an assumption-by-MC prediction matrix. Compared against T081 (MC2+MC3+MC4 generative simulation), T082 (improved MC2+MC3+MC4 simulation), and T082+persistence (SP/MC5/MC1). Tested three explanations per assumption.

### Divergence Pattern

The 9 assumptions fall into 3 clean groups:

#### Group 1: Confirmed (Match — 2/9)
IS1 emerges strongly in both T081 (+0.772) and T082 (+0.687). **MC3 (Constraint Balance) generates Phase Structure robustly.** IC1 emerges in T082 (+0.341). These are the program's strongest evidence for MC→substrate generation.

#### Group 2: Over-mapped by T080 (H1 — 3/9)
OC1 (−0.399, −0.319), IS2 (−0.450, −0.438), EC1 (−0.044, +0.312) have NEGATIVE or near-zero deltas from MC2+MC3+MC4 alone. Despite MC3's +1 score for OC1 and MC2's +1 for IS2, the generative simulation shows these assumptions are **actively suppressed** by the transformation-and-balance regime. They only emerge when a persistence constraint (SP/MC5/MC1) is added.

Of these:
- **OC1 and IS2 are genuine T080 mapping errors.** MC3 does NOT generate stable structure; MC2 does NOT generate determinate outputs. Both are anti-correlated with the transformation-balance regime. A persistence constraint is required.
- **EC1 is borderline.** T081 δ=−0.044 (essentially zero), T082 δ=+0.312 (emerges at lower threshold). The MC4→EC1 mapping is weak but not entirely absent.

#### Group 3: Simulation-limited (H2 — 4/9)
OC2 (+0.121, +0.023), CD2 (+0.085, +0.343), SR1 (+0.163, +0.560), CD1 (−0.249, −0.222) show weak-to-moderate emergence in T081 but stronger emergence in T082.

- **OC2** (distinguishability, from MC3): Very weak emergence (+0.121 in T081, +0.023 in T082). The "minimal constraint" claim is supported directionally but the effect is tiny. OC2 may be a presupposition of MC3 rather than a product — you can't have constraint balance without something to constrain.
- **CD2** and **SR1** emerge clearly in T082 (+0.343, +0.560) but not T081 (+0.085, +0.163). The improved simulation reveals genuine generation that T081's narrower sampling missed.
- **CD1** (causal relations): Not predicted by T080's scores from MC234 (needs MC1). T081/T82 both negative. The emergence with persistence (+0.524 in SP) confirms CD1 needs a persistence constraint.

### Phase 5: Forced Verdict

**GENUINE THEORETICAL GAP** — but highly specific:

**T080 correctly identified that MC2 and MC3 are the generative core.** IS1 (phase structure) emerges robustly from both. IC1 (extractable info) emerges from T082. The mapping works for transformation-balance generative effects.

**T080 incorrectly attributed OC1 and IS2 to MC2/MC3.** These assumptions are anti-correlated with transformation-and-balance dynamics. They require a persistence-class constraint (SP/MC5/MC1) that T080 identified as optional or absent (MC1 score +1 for IS2, but not in the "derivation from MC2+MC3" claim).

**The specific theoretical gap is:**

> T080 claims MC2+MC3 generate 8/9 assumptions. The generative evidence shows MC2+MC3 generate 2/9 (IS1, IC1) robustly, generate 3/9 weakly (OC2, CD2, SR1 — only in T082), and 3/9 not at all (OC1, IS2, EC1 — require persistence).

**Resolution: The working synthesis is confirmed.**

> Transformation (MC2) and Balance (MC3) generate viability conditions (IC1, IS1) and weak structural precursors (OC2, CD2, SR1). Persistence-class constraints (SP/MC5/MC1) are required for structural realization (OC1, OC2, CD1, IS2, EC1, SR1).

The T080 ↔ T081/T082 contradiction is resolved by recognizing that T080's "MC2+MC3 generate 8/9" conflated two different generative functions:
1. **Viability generation** (MC2+MC3 → IC1, IS1, weak CD2/SR1)
2. **Structural realization** (MC2+MC3+persistence → ALL 9 assumptions)

T080 treated these as one process. The generative simulations show they are two experimentally separable processes (as T082 originally demonstrated).

## Conclusion
The mapping error is that T080 under-specified the persistence requirement. The T080 scores included MC1 as a predictor for OC1, IC1, IS2, CD1, but the "MC2+MC3 generate 8/9" narrative excluded MC1 from the generative set. The generative simulations show MC1/SP/MC5 is structurally necessary for 5/9 assumptions (OC1, CD1, IS2, EC1, SR1) and beneficial for the rest.

**This is not a simulation error, not a threshold error. It is a mapping error — but a narrow and precise one.** T080's individual MC-assignment scores are largely correct. T080's synthetic claim ("MC2+MC3+MC4 generate the substrate") overstates the independence of the transformation-balance regime from persistence constraints.

The strongest supported statement is:

> MC2+MC3+MC4 generate viability conditions (IC1, IS1, weak CD2/SR1) and presuppose OC2. Adding a persistence constraint (SP-class) completes structural realization (OC1, OC2, CD1, IS2, EC1, SR1, full CD2/SR1). Neither regime alone suffices.
