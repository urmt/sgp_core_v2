================================================================================
SECTION 1: OPERATIONAL DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

A. RELATIONAL STATE
--------------------
Definition: A recursively persistent organizational configuration.

Operationalization:
- Identify stable trajectory clusters across depths
- State = cluster_center + bounded_variance
- Persistence = cluster retention across depth

B. TRANSITION EVENT
--------------------
Definition: A measurable change between persistent organizational
configurations.

Operationalization:
- Compute trajectory state changes
- transition = distance between state_centers
- Count transitions per depth unit

C. METASTABLE TRANSITION
-------------------------
Definition: Transition between bounded persistent regions without
diffuse escape.

Operationalization:
- Check transition boundedness
- metastable = transitions bounded + retention high
- Diffuse = unbounded transitions or no retention

D. TRANSITION RETENTION
------------------------
Definition: Persistence of recognizable transition pathways
across recursive depth.

Operationalization:
- Track transition pathway similarity across depths
- retention = correlation(pathway_d, pathway_d+k)
- Higher retention = more stable pathways

E. SECTOR TRANSITION BIAS
----------------------------
Definition: Sector-dependent asymmetry in transition behavior.

Operationalization:
- Compare transition rates across sectors
- bias = |rate_A - rate_B| / avg_rate
- Higher bias = more asymmetric

F. TRANSITION STABILITY
-------------------------
Definition: Bounded variability of transition structure across
recursive scales.

Operationalization:
- Compute variance of transition metrics across depths
- Stability = 1 - normalized_variance
- Higher stability = more bounded
