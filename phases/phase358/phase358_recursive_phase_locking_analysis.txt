8. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

H1 (SYNCHRONIZATION STABILITY > 0.75): PASS
- P-A-N, P-A, Projection achieve phase-locked structure

H2 (DESYNCHRONIZATION RESISTANCE > 0.70): PARTIAL
- P-A-N maintains > 0.70 through depth 20
- A-N collapses before depth 8

H3 (COHERENCE DRIFT < 0.30): PARTIAL
- P-A-N maintains < 0.30 through depth 20
- Neutral exceeds from depth 12

H4 (RG SIMILARITY > 0.90): FAIL
- Only P-A-N at depth 1 achieves > 0.90
- Scale invariance limited to strong networks at early depths

H5 (PHASE-LOCKING HIERARCHY): PASS
- P-A-N > P-A > P-N > A-N preserved across depths

OVERALL VERDICT: WEAKLY_SYNCHRONIZED
- Phase-locking stability exists in strong networks (P-A-N, P-A)
- Desynchronization resistance maintained for P-A-N through depth 20
- Coherence drift bounded for P-A-N
- Weak systems collapse
- Scale invariance limited
