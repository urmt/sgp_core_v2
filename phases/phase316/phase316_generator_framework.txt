================================================================================
A. GENERATOR STRUCTURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
A minimal subset capable of recursively reconstructing broader organization.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which a subset of organizational elements can generate
the full organizational structure through recursive composition.

QUANTIFICATION:
Generator strength measures the coverage of the full structure by the
minimal generating subset.

================================================================================
B. GENERATOR EFFICIENCY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Ratio of organizational recovery to generator size.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The coverage achieved divided by the number of elements in the generator
subset relative to the full structure.

QUANTIFICATION:
Generator efficiency = (coverage) / (size_ratio)

================================================================================
C. RECURSIVE BASIS STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of generating subsets across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which the minimal generating subset remains stable as
recursive composition depth increases.

QUANTIFICATION:
Basis stability measures the overlap of generators at different depths.

================================================================================
D. GENERATOR DISPERSION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Variance of generator contribution across sectors.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree of variance in how generator elements contribute to different
sectors of the organizational structure.

QUANTIFICATION:
Dispersion measures the heterogeneity of generator contribution.

================================================================================
E. RG GENERATOR INVARIANCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Stability of generator structure under coarse-graining.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which the generator subset remains valid after
coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity measures the correlation between fine and coarse generators.

================================================================================
GENERATOR FRAMEWORK FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

GENERATOR = (G_str, G_eff, B_stab, G_disp, RG_sim)

Where:
- G_str: Generator strength
- G_eff: Generator efficiency
- B_stab: Basis stability
- G_disp: Generator dispersion
- RG_sim: RG similarity
