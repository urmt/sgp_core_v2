CONFIRMATION: NO ONTOLOGY
================================================================================

Phase 326 does NOT introduce ontology. The recursive channel framework
studies:
- Recursive channel as organizational propagation pathway
- Organizational propagation as cross-depth influence transfer
- Channel persistence as pathway stability
- Bounded channel drift as controlled variance

No ontological claims are made about:
- Communication systems
- Semantic signaling
- Observer persistence
- Memory or cognition
- Physical transport
- Idealism or physical reality
- Network or field theory
