   REASON: This interpretation assumes that channel persistence corresponds

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that propagation retention
   corresponds to memory retention, cognitive persistence, or mental
   storage. Phase 326 does not study memory, cognition, or mental
   processes.

5. "channel drift indicates physical transport"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that channel drift corresponds
   to physical transport, particle flow, or material transfer.
   Phase 326 does not study physical transport or material flow.

6. "channel structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that channel structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 326 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "bounded channel drift proves network or field theory"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to network dynamics, field theory, or graph structure.
   Phase 326 does not study networks, fields, or graphs.
