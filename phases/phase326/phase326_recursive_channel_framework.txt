================================================================================
A. RECURSIVE CHANNEL
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Organizational pathway through which recursive influence propagates
from one recursive depth to another.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Channel strength measures the degree to which organizational
influence propagates through persistent rather than diffuse paths.

QUANTIFICATION:
Channel strength = propagation_coherence / max_propagation

================================================================================
B. ORGANIZATIONAL PROPAGATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational influence transfer across recursive depths.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Propagation retention quantifies the degree to which organizational
properties are transmitted through the channel.

QUANTIFICATION:
Propagation retention = influence_at_depth_n / influence_at_depth_n-1

================================================================================
C. CHANNEL PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of channel stability across recursive depth evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Channel persistence coefficient quantifies the degree to which
propagation pathways remain stable.

QUANTIFICATION:
Persistence = correlation(channel_depth_n, channel_depth_n-1)

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED CHANNEL DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of channel structure
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which channel drift remains controlled rather
than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Channel drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG CHANNEL PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of recursive channel structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which channel organization survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_channel_metrics, coarse_channel_metrics)
