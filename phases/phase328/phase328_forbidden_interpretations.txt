================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "organizational integration proves cognitive integration"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational integration
   corresponds to cognitive integration, neural binding, or mental
   unification. Phase 328 does not study cognition, neural systems,
   or mental processes.

2. "coordination retention implies consciousness unity"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that coordination retention
   corresponds to consciousness unity, unified awareness, or subjective
   experience. Phase 328 does not study consciousness or subjective
   experience.

3. "integration strength implies semantic binding"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that integration strength
   corresponds to semantic binding, linguistic unity, or meaning
   integration. Phase 328 does not study semantics or language.

4. "integration preservation implies observer unification"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that integration preservation
   corresponds to observer unification, identity persistence, or
   self-coherence. Phase 328 does not study observers or identity.

5. "bounded integration drift proves physical field unification"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to physical field unification, gauge symmetry, or force unification.
   Phase 328 does not study physical fields or unification.

6. "integration structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that integration structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 328 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "recursive coordination implies quantum coherence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive coordination
   corresponds to quantum coherence, quantum entanglement, or quantum
   information processing. Phase 328 does not study quantum systems.
