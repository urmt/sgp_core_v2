================================================================================
A. ORGANIZATIONAL INTEGRATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of coordination and coherence between multiple recursive
organizational components.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Integration strength measures the degree to which organizational
components maintain coherent coordination rather than fragmenting.

QUANTIFICATION:
Integration strength = coordination_coherence / max_coherence

================================================================================
B. RECURSIVE COORDINATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Mutual adjustment and alignment of organizational components
across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Coordination retention quantifies the degree to which integration
is preserved through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coordination retention = integration_depth_n / integration_depth_n-1

================================================================================
C. INTEGRATION RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational integration persistence across
recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Retention coefficient quantifies the degree to which coordinated
organization remains stable.

QUANTIFICATION:
Retention = correlation(integration_across_depths)

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED INTEGRATION DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of organizational integration
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which integration drift remains controlled rather
than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Integration drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG INTEGRATION PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of organizational integration structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which integration organization survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_integration_metrics, coarse_integration_metrics)
