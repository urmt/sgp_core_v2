================================================================================
A. RG CLOSURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
A recursive structure whose organizational relations remain stable under
repeated coarse-graining operations.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which sector relations, hierarchy structure, and universality
patterns persist through multiple RG iterations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Closure strength measures the persistence of organizational structure under
recursive scale transformation.

================================================================================
B. STRUCTURAL COLLAPSE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Loss of distinguishable recursive organization under RG iteration.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The rate at which recursive structures become indistinguishable or diffuse
under repeated coarse-graining.

QUANTIFICATION:
Collapse indicator measures the degradation of organizational structure
through RG iterations.

================================================================================
C. HIERARCHICAL PRESERVATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Retention of sector ordering and recursive structure across scales.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which hierarchical relationships between sectors remain
unchanged under RG transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Hierarchy preservation measures the stability of sector hierarchy across
coarse-graining operations.

================================================================================
D. RECURSIVE UNIVERSALITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-independent persistence of organizational classifications.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which universality patterns established in prior phases
survive repeated RG transformations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Universality retention measures the preservation of implementation-
independent organizational structure.

================================================================================
E. CLOSURE STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Bounded deviation of recursive structure under repeated RG transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which closure strength remains bounded and nonzero through
multiple RG iterations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Structural drift measures the deviation of recursive organization from
initial stable state under RG iteration.

================================================================================
CLOSURE STRUCTURE FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

RG_CLOSURE = (C_str, H_pres, U_ret, S_drift, R_stab)

Where:
- C_str: Closure strength
- H_pres: Hierarchy preservation
- U_ret: Universality retention
- S_drift: Structural drift
- R_stab: Recursive stability

================================================================================
HYPOTHESES
================================================================================

H1: Closure strength remains > 0.50 across RG iterations
H2: Hierarchy preservation remains > 0.50
H3: Structural drift remains bounded (< 1.00)
H4: RG stability correlation remains > 0.90
H5: Recursive organization remains nonzero across all sectors
