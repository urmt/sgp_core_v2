## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 394 analyzes recursive dynamical law emergence and stable evolution regimes as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All references to "law," "invariance," "evolution," "dynamical," and "regime" are strictly mathematical — describing organizational patterns in recursive relations, not physical laws or natural necessity. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
