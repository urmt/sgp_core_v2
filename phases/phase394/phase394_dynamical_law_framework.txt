# Phase 394 Framework: Recursive Dynamical Law Emergence and Stable Evolution Regimes

## Purpose
Determine whether emergent recursive causal-temporal organization can generate stable law-like dynamical evolution regimes from recursive relational propagation constraints alone.

## Core Distinction
emergent causal-temporal organization vs stable recursive dynamical law emergence

## Structure

### 1. Recursive Evolution Regularity Extraction
- Extract stable evolution regularities from recursive dynamics
- Measure dynamical consistency (DC)
- Identify regularity-stable regimes

### 2. Law-Like Invariance Tracking
- Track invariance patterns across recursive evolution
- Measure law invariance stability (LIS)
- Identify invariant transformation structures

### 3. Propagation-Rule Persistence Analysis
- Analyze persistence of propagation-based rules
- Measure recursive evolution persistence (REP)
- Identify rule degradation patterns

### 4. Recursive Dynamical Consistency Mapping
- Map dynamical consistency across recursion depths
- Measure transformation reproducibility (TR)
- Identify consistency-stable regimes

### 5. Causal Transformation Reproducibility
- Track reproducibility of causal transformations
- Measure causal dynamical alignment (CDA)
- Identify reproducible transformation regimes

### 6. Evolution Regime Stabilization
- Analyze stabilization of evolution regimes
- Measure evolution regime gain (ERG)
- Identify stabilization thresholds

### 7. Null Dynamical Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm dynamical law signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- dynamical_consistency (DC)
- law_invariance_stability (LIS)
- recursive_evolution_persistence (REP)
- transformation_reproducibility (TR)
- causal_dynamical_alignment (CDA)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- evolution_regime_gain (ERG)

## Classification Thresholds
- DYNAMICAL-LAW-STABLE: DC > 0.90, LIS > 0.85, REP > 0.85
- DYNAMICAL-LAW-BOUNDED: DC > 0.80, LIS > 0.75, REP > 0.75
- DYNAMICAL-LAW-PARTIAL: DC > 0.70, LIS > 0.65, REP > 0.65
- DYNAMICAL-LAW-DEGRADING: DC > 0.55
- DYNAMICAL-LAW-FAILED: DC <= 0.55
