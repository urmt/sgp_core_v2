STRICT INTERPRETATION CONSTRAINTS
================================================================================

results. These interpretations would violate the empirical and mathematical
constraints established in prior phases.

================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "core-periphery structure proves physical field emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that core-periphery structure
   corresponds to physical field emergence, force distributions, or
   energy concentration. Phase 321 does not study physical fields,
   energy distributions, or force structures.

2. "core structure implies consciousness or awareness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that core structure corresponds
   to consciousness, awareness, or subjective experience. Phase 321 does
   not study consciousness, awareness, or subjective phenomena.

3. "peripheral drift proves cognitive peripheral processing"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that peripheral drift corresponds
   to cognitive peripheral processing, attention mechanisms, or neural
   distribution. Phase 321 does not study cognition, attention, or
   neural systems.

4. "recursive centrality implies observer centrality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive centrality
   corresponds to observer centrality, perspective, or measurement
   frame. Phase 321 does not study observers, measurement, or
   perspective.

5. "core-periphery structure implies semantic architecture"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that core-periphery structure
   corresponds to semantic meaning, linguistic hierarchy, or information
   architecture. Phase 321 does not study semantics, meaning, or
   language.

6. "bounded drift indicates spatial emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to spatial emergence, dimensional structure, or geometric formation.
   Phase 321 does not study space, dimensions, or geometry.

7. "core structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that core structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 321 does
   not study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.
