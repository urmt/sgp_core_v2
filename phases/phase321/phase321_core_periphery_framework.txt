A. CORE STRUCTURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:

core-like organizational properties.

QUANTIFICATION:
Core strength = normalized centrality × structural persistence

================================================================================
B. PERIPHERAL STRUCTURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Organizational region exhibiting weaker persistence and
displaced position within recursive relational structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Peripheral drift measures the degree of displacement from
core-like organizational behavior.

QUANTIFICATION:
Peripheral drift = 1 - (core_strength / max_core_strength)

================================================================================
C. RECURSIVE CENTRALITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of a sector's central organizational position within
the recursive relational structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Centrality quantifies the degree to which a sector occupies
the core position relative to other sectors.

QUANTIFICATION:
Centrality = structural coherence / mean_structural_coherence

================================================================================

Constraint on unbounded divergence of peripheral sectors

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which peripheral drift remains controlled
rather than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Peripheral drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
