# Phase 385 Framework: Recursive Predictive Organization and Anticipatory Stabilization

## Purpose
Determine whether historically encoded recursive organizational systems can develop predictive stabilization behavior that anticipates future recursive perturbations and reorganizations.

## Structure

### 1. Predictive Stabilization Analysis
- Analyze predictive stabilization patterns
- Measure predictive stability (PS)
- Identify stabilization anticipation thresholds

### 2. Anticipatory Routing Tracking
- Track anticipatory transport routing
- Measure anticipatory coherence (AC)
- Identify routing prediction patterns

### 3. Perturbation Forecasting Evaluation
- Evaluate perturbation forecasting accuracy
- Measure perturbation forecast accuracy (PFA)
- Identify forecast precision patterns

### 4. Future-State Reconstruction
- Reconstruct future organizational states from present structure
- Measure future state alignment (FSA)
- Identify reconstruction fidelity

### 5. Adaptive Predictive Modulation
- Track adaptive modulation of predictive behavior
- Measure adaptive anticipation gain (AAG)
- Identify modulation dynamics

### 6. Recursive Anticipation Persistence
- Track anticipation persistence across recursive depths
- Measure recursive prediction persistence (RPP)
- Identify persistence degradation patterns

### 7. Null Predictive Controls
- Randomized predictive baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- predictive_stability (PS)
- anticipatory_coherence (AC)
- perturbation_forecast_accuracy (PFA)
- future_state_alignment (FSA)
- recursive_prediction_persistence (RPP)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- adaptive_anticipation_gain (AAG)

## Classification Thresholds
- PREDICTIVE-STABLE: PS > 0.90, AC > 0.90, PFA > 0.85
- PREDICTIVE-BOUNDED: PS > 0.80, AC > 0.80, PFA > 0.75
- PREDICTIVE-PARTIAL: PS > 0.70, AC > 0.70, PFA > 0.65
- PREDICTIVE-DEGRADING: PS > 0.55
- PREDICTIVE-FAILED: PS <= 0.55
