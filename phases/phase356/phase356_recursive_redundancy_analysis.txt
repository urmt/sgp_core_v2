8. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

H1 (REDUNDANCY RETENTION > 0.75): PASS
- P-A-N, P-A, Projection achieve redundant stability

- P-A-N maintains > 0.70 through depth 20
- A-N collapses before depth 8

H3 (FAILURE CONTAINMENT > 0.70): PARTIAL
- P-A-N maintains > 0.70 through depth 8

H4 (RG SIMILARITY > 0.90): FAIL

- Scale invariance limited to strong networks at early depths

H5 (REDUNDANCY HIERARCHY): PASS
- P-A-N > P-A > P-N > A-N preserved across depths

OVERALL VERDICT: WEAKLY_REDUNDANT
- Redundant stability exists in strong networks (P-A-N, P-A)
- Compensation strength maintained for P-A-N through depth 20
- Failure containment bounded for P-A-N at early depths
- Weak systems collapse
- Scale invariance limited
