================================================================================
A. EMBEDDING CONTINUITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of relational placement within organizational structure
across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Embedding strength measures the degree to which organizational
relations maintain consistent relative positioning.

QUANTIFICATION:
Embedding strength = placement_consistency / max_consistency

================================================================================
B. EMBEDDING DEFORMATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Magnitude of organizational displacement from baseline placement
under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Embedding deformation quantifies the degree of relational
displacement across recursive depth.

QUANTIFICATION:
Deformation = displacement_magnitude / baseline_scale

================================================================================
C. CROSS-DEPTH ALIGNMENT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Similarity between shallow depth and deep depth embedding relations.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Cross-depth alignment measures the preservation of relational
structure across depth ranges.

QUANTIFICATION:
Alignment = correlation(shallow_embedding, deep_embedding)

================================================================================
D. EMBEDDING RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Fraction of organizational relations preserving relative placement
through recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Retention coefficient quantifies the fraction of embedding
structures surviving transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Retention = preserved_relations / total_relations

================================================================================
E. RECURSIVE EMBEDDING STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of embedding organization under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Embedding stability measures the degree to which relational
placement survives recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability = 1 - deformation_variance / max_variance

================================================================================
EMBEDDING FRAMEWORK FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

E_STRUCTURE = (E_str, E_def, C_align, E_ret, RG_sim)

Where:
- E_str: Embedding strength
- E_def: Embedding deformation
- C_align: Cross-depth alignment
- E_ret: Embedding retention
- RG_sim: RG similarity
