   REASON: This interpretation assumes that attractor strength

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that basin drift corresponds

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that attractor-basin structure
   corresponds to semantic meaning, linguistic basins, or information

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that attractor structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 324 does not
