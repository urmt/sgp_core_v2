================================================================================
A. ATTRACTOR REGION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Organizational region toward which recursive trajectories
converge under repeated transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Attractor strength measures the degree to which organizational
trajectories converge toward stable regions.

QUANTIFICATION:
Attractor strength = convergence_rate / max_convergence_rate

================================================================================
B. BASIN ORGANIZATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Set of organizational states that converge toward a given
attractor region under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Basin convergence measures the degree to which organizational
states within the basin converge to the attractor.

QUANTIFICATION:
Basin convergence = mean_convergence(initial_states, attractor)

================================================================================
C. RECURSIVE CONVERGENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational trajectory convergence across
recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Convergence coefficient quantifies the rate at which recursive
trajectories approach attractor regions.

QUANTIFICATION:
Convergence = 1 - variance(trajectory_positions) / variance(initial)

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED BASIN DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of basin boundaries
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which basin drift remains controlled rather
than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Basin drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG ATTRACTOR PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of attractor-basin structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which attractor-basin organization survives
coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_attractor_metrics, coarse_attractor_metrics)
