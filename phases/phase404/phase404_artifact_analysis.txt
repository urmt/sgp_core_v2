4. WHAT CAN REPRODUCE THE HIERARCHY?

Non-trivial hierarchy reproduction requires one of:
(a) Order-preserving transformations (RecursiveOrderInversion):
    Inverting depth order preserves relative sector ordering because
    all sectors scale by the same depth factor. The hierarchy is
    invariant under monotonic depth transformations.

(b) Explicit value construction (AdversarialHierarchySpoofing,
    AdversarialOptimizationTowardHierarchy): Directly assigning
    hierarchy-ordered values trivially reproduces the pattern.

(c) Decay-preserved ordering (FakePropagationDynamics): Exponential
    decay preserves relative ordering across sectors because each
    sector's value scales proportionally.

These are all mechanisms that PRESERVE or CONSTRUCT the relative
ordering between sectors. They do not "discover" or "generate" it
naturally — they encode it explicitly.

5. WHAT CANNOT REPRODUCE THE HIERARCHY?

Random processes consistently fail:
- RandomizedMetricScrambling: random permutation destroys ordering
- CorrelatedRandomNoiseSystems: noise scrambles fine sector differences
- HiddenDepthEncodingInjection: depth-only signal cannot produce
  sector differentiation
- BiasedInitializationInjection: even with hierarchy-biased initialization,
  randomness dominates

Structural modifications also fail:
- SectorLabelPermutation: reverses the hierarchy (P-A-N gets A-N values)
- RecursiveSymmetryCorruption: degrades ordering (P-A-N still highest
  but other sectors scrambled)
- PartialEmergentSignatureMimicry: partial authenticity not sufficient
  to preserve full ordering

6. CRITICAL DISTINCTION: NATURAL vs CONSTRUCTED

The authentic emergence (Phase 391-403) generates the hierarchy
NATURALLY from relational propagation constraints — no explicit
hierarchy construction, no optimization, no value assignment.

The artifact spoofs require EXPLICIT construction — someone must
deliberately assign hierarchy-ordered values (spoofing, optimization)
or apply order-preserving transformations (inversion, decay).

This is the fundamental distinction:
  Authentic emergence: hierarchy falls out of the dynamics
  Artifact reproduction: hierarchy must be explicitly put in
