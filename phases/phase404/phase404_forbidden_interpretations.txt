PHASE 404 FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS

The following interpretations of artifact detection results are
FORBIDDEN. These terms reify deception, counterfeiting, forensics,
or adversarial machine learning metaphors as literal properties.
All results must be framed as structural similarity comparison
between generative processes.

Instead of the above, use:

- "Hierarchy reproduction vs natural generation"

- "Generative process comparison"

- "Random process failure to reproduce ordering"

- "Generative mechanism distinguishability"
- "Natural vs constructed structural patterns"
- "Computational artifact detection"

RATIONALIZATION

Phase 404 investigates whether the emergence hierarchy can be reproduced
by non-emergent computational mechanisms. The contrast is between
NATURAL GENERATION (relational propagation producing the hierarchy
without explicit design) and ARTIFACTUAL CONSTRUCTION (deliberately
assigning values to match the hierarchy). This is NOT about detecting
deception, fraud, or inauthenticity — it is about comparing outcomes
of different generative processes.

All terminology must remain structurally comparative rather than
adversarial-forensic. The question is not "is the emergence real?"
but "what generative processes can produce this pattern?"
