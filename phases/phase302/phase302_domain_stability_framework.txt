# PHASE 302: DOMAIN STABILITY FRAMEWORK
# Emergent Relational Domain Stability
# STRICTLY MATHEMATICAL | NO COGNITION | NO CONSCIOUSNESS

================================================================================
A. ORGANIZATIONAL DOMAIN
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
A bounded recursive region exhibiting persistent internal organizational structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which recursive geometry exhibits localized organizational
coherence that forms distinct bounded regions.

QUANTIFICATION:
Domain strength measures the coherence of organizational structure within
bounded regions.

================================================================================
B. DOMAIN STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Retention of bounded recursive organization under propagation and perturbation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which domain boundaries and internal structure persist through
recursive transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Internal stability measures the preservation of domain structure.

================================================================================
C. DOMAIN RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of identifiable domain structure across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which domain structure remains identifiable as recursion
increases.

QUANTIFICATION:
Recursive retention measures the persistence of domain identity.

================================================================================
D. DOMAIN INTERACTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Recursive influence between bounded organizational regions.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which adjacent domains influence each other's organizational
structure.

QUANTIFICATION:
Interaction strength measures cross-domain influence.

================================================================================
E. DOMAIN BOUNDEDNESS
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded recursive dispersion.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which recursive organization remains contained within
bounded regions rather than dispersing infinitely.

QUANTIFICATION:
Boundedness measures the containment of organizational structure.
Dispersion measures the degree of unbounded spread.

================================================================================
F. DOMAIN RG STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of domain organization.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which domain structure survives coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG stability measures the correlation between fine and coarse domain metrics.

================================================================================
DOMAIN STRUCTURE FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

DOMAIN = (D_str, I_stab, I_int, Bound, D_ret, Disp, RG_stab)

Where:
- D_str: Domain strength
- I_stab: Internal stability
- I_int: Interaction strength
- Bound: Boundedness
- D_ret: Domain retention
- Disp: Dispersion
- RG_stab: RG stability

================================================================================
HYPOTHESES
================================================================================

H1: Domain strength > 0.50
H2: Internal stability > 0.50
H3: Dispersion < 1.00 (bounded)
H4: Recursive retention > 0 across all sectors
H5: RG stability > 0.90

================================================================================
SECTOR DOMAIN CLASSIFICATIONS
================================================================================

DOMAIN-PRESERVING: Strong domains (strength > 0.70, stability > 0.70)
DOMAIN-NEUTRAL: Moderate domains (strength > 0.50, stability > 0.50)
METASTABLE-DOMAIN: Marginal domains (strength > 0.30, stability > 0.30)
DIFFUSE: Weak domains (strength < 0.30)
DOMAIN-SUPPRESSING: No domains (strength < 0.10)
