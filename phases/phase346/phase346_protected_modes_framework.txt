THEORETICAL FRAMEWORK: PROTECTED MODES
================================================================================

Protected recursive organizational mode:
- Certain recursive structures remain stable despite perturbation
- Resistance to recursive degradation pressure
- Bounded perturbation spread
- Recursive shielding mechanism

Key distinction from prior phases:
- STABILITY (Phase 280+): Baseline maintenance
- LOCALIZATION (Phase 344): Regional concentration
- CORRELATION (Phase 345): Cross-domain co-variation
- PROTECTION (Phase 346): Active resistance to degradation

Protection measures resistance to disruption.
Stability measures return to equilibrium.
Localization measures regional concentration.
Correlation measures cross-domain co-variation.

================================================================================
METRIC DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

PROTECTION_STRENGTH:
- Degree of resistance to perturbative degradation
- Range: [0, 1]
- High values indicate strong protection
- Values > 0.70 indicate protected modes

MODE_RETENTION:
- Fraction of organizational mode surviving perturbation
- Measures persistence under disruption
- Values > 0.70 indicate good retention

PERTURBATION_DISPERSION:
- Spread of disruption across recursive structure
- Range: [0, 1]
- Low values indicate bounded spread
- Values < 0.30 indicate good containment

SHIELDING_STABILITY:
- Consistency of degradation propagation bounding
- Measures recursive shielding effectiveness
- Values > 0.70 indicate stable shielding

RECURSIVE_PROTECTION:
- Protection persistence across recursive depths
- Measures depth-dependent protection
- Values > 0.70 indicate persistent protection

RG_SIMILARITY:
- Fine-grained vs coarse-grained protection preservation
- > 0.90 indicates stable scale invariance
