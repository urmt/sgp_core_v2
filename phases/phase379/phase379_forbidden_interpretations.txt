# Phase 379 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Learning, adaptation, memory, recognition
- Meaning, significance, interpretation, representation
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)
- Evolution, emergence (beyond structural pattern description)
- Intelligence, wisdom, knowledge
- Identity, self, personality, character

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Conservation as "protection" or "preservation" with intent
- Invariant preservation as "memory" or "legacy"
- Redistribution as "sharing" or "fairness"
- Perturbation recovery as "healing" or "resilience"
- Transport conservation as "delivery guarantee"
- Attractor-linked conservation as "anchoring" or "grounding"
- Scale-independent conservation as "eternal truth" or "universal law"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric, topological, algebraic
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 379 analyzes organizational conservation structure as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
