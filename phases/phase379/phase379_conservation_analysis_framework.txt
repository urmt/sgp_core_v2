# Phase 379 Framework: Universal Organizational Conservation Analysis

## Purpose
Determine whether scale-independent recursive organizational systems develop conserved organizational quantities that persist across recursive evolution, perturbation, transport, and rescaling.

## Structure

### 1. Recursive Conservation Tracking
- Track conserved quantities across recursive depths
- Measure conservation stability (CS)
- Identify conservation emergence patterns

### 2. Invariant Redistribution Analysis
- Track invariant redistribution under perturbation
- Measure invariant preservation index (IPI)
- Identify redistribution conservation patterns

### 3. Perturbation Conservation Recovery
- Apply perturbations and track conservation recovery
- Measure perturbation conservation recovery (PCR)
- Identify conservation resilience thresholds

### 4. Transport Conservation Persistence
- Track conservation under transport dynamics
- Measure recursive conservation persistence (RCP)
- Identify transport-conservation coupling

### 5. Attractor-Linked Conservation Mapping
- Map conservation quantities to attractor topology
- Measure attractor conservation alignment (ACA)
- Identify topology-linked conservation laws

### 6. Scale-Independent Conservation Testing
- Test conservation under arbitrary scale transformation
- Measure universality conservation score (UCS)
- Identify scale-independent invariants

### 7. Null Conservation Controls
- Randomized conservation baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- conservation_stability (CS)
- invariant_preservation_index (IPI)
- redistribution_coherence (RC)
- recursive_conservation_persistence (RCP)
- attractor_conservation_alignment (ACA)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- universality_conservation_score (UCS)

## Classification Thresholds
- CONSERVATION-STABLE: CS > 0.85, IPI > 0.85, RCP > 0.80
- CONSERVATION-BOUNDED: CS > 0.75, IPI > 0.75, RCP > 0.70
- CONSERVATION-PARTIAL: CS > 0.65, IPI > 0.65, RCP > 0.60
- CONSERVATION-DEGRADING: CS > 0.50
- CONSERVATION-FAILED: CS <= 0.50
