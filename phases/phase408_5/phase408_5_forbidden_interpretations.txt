# Phase 408.5: Forbidden Interpretations

## STRICTLY FORBIDDEN

1. **"17.24% OOD MAPE means prediction is universal."** OOD prediction
   is strong but not universal. Two failure modes exist (inversion,
   periodic recursion). Universality is bounded, not absolute.

2. **"Inversion operator failure disproves the framework."** The
   predictor assumes positive operators (Axiom 1). Inversion violates
   this axiom. Failure is expected, not contradictory.

3. **"Periodic recursion failure means depth analysis is wrong."**
   Periodic recursion violates monotonic depth decay (Axiom 3).
   Failure confirms the axiom, it doesn't disprove depth analysis.

4. **"AlienArchitectures 1.5% MAPE proves sector count doesn't
   matter."** It proves the predictor generalizes across sector
   counts for similar structures. Extremely different architectures
   (e.g., no sectors) were not tested.

5. **"OOD generalization = physical universality."** OOD success
   demonstrates mathematical generalization within the relational
   emergence framework, not physical law universality.
