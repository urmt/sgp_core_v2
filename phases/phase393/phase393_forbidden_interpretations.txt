# Phase 393 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Time, temporality (beyond relational ordering), causality (beyond structural dependency)
- Past, present, future (beyond ordinal relation)
- Before, after (beyond structural precedence)
- Arrow of time, temporal flow, temporal passage
- Memory, anticipation, expectation
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)
- Evolution, emergence (beyond structural pattern description)

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Causal ordering as "physical causation" or "causal power"
- Temporal consistency as "time keeping" or "temporal experience"
- Propagation asymmetry as "arrow of time" or "temporal direction"
- Causal chain as "chain of events" or "causal history"
- Transport direction as "temporal passage" or "flow of time"
- Temporal alignment as "synchronization" or "temporal coordination"
- Causal-temporal as "time" or "causality"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric, topological, algebraic
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 393 analyzes recursive causal structure and temporal order emergence as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All references to "causal," "temporal," "before/after," and "ordering" are strictly relational — describing organizational patterns in recursive relations, not physical time or causality. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
