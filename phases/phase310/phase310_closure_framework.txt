================================================================================
A. RECURSIVE CLOSURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistent recursive organizational structure remaining within bounded
universality regimes under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which compositional organizational structure remains
within bounded universality classes when subjected to recursive
transformation operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Closure strength measures the maintenance of bounded organizational
structure under recursive transformations.

================================================================================
B. CLOSURE STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Retention of organizational identity under recursive transformation
composition.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which closure structure remains identifiable and
consistent when transformations are composed.

QUANTIFICATION:
Closure stability measures the preservation of closure identity.

================================================================================
C. CLOSURE RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of identifiable closure structure across recursive
evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which closure organization maintains its defining
properties as recursive transformations accumulate.

QUANTIFICATION:
Recursive retention measures the persistence of closure structure.

================================================================================
D. CLOSURE TRANSITION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Bounded transition between closure-preserving regimes.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which transitions between closure states remain
bounded and controlled rather than abrupt or catastrophic.

QUANTIFICATION:
Transition control measures the boundedness of closure changes.

================================================================================
E. CLOSURE BOUNDEDNESS
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence during recursive transformation
propagation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which recursive transformations maintain bounded
structure without uncontrolled expansion or collapse.

QUANTIFICATION:
Boundedness measures the containment of closure organization.
Dispersion measures the degree of unbounded spread.

================================================================================
F. CLOSURE RG STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of closure organization.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which closure structure survives coarse-graining
operations at different scales.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG stability measures the correlation between fine and coarse
closure metrics.
