FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that closure organization implies
   physical conservation. Phase 310 does not study conservation laws,
   energy preservation, or physical invariance. We study organizational
   closure within a mathematical framework.

2. "recursive closure proves physics"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive closure corresponds
   to physical processes. Phase 310 does not study physical systems.
   We study persistence and stability within a mathematical model.

3. "stable closure proves matter persistence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable closure organization
   implies material persistence. Phase 310 does not address questions of
   matter, substance, or material continuity. We study organizational
   closure within empirical constraints.

4. "RG closure proves spacetime"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   closure stability implies the existence of physical spacetime. Phase 310
   does not study spacetime geometry, physical dimensions, or space-time
   structure. We study scale invariance within a mathematical framework.

5. "organizational closure is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that closure organization corresponds
   to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 310 does not study universe
   formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study closure stability
   within empirical constraints.

6. "closure strength implies fundamental forces"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that closure metrics correspond to
   fundamental physical forces. Phase 310 does not study forces, interactions,
   or field theory. We study organizational structure within a mathematical
   model.

7. "transformation stability implies physical causality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that transformation stability
   corresponds to physical causality. Phase 310 does not study causality,
   cause-effect relationships, or physical mechanisms. We study identity
   preservation under transformation operations.
