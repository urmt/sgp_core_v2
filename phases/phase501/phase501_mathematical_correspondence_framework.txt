# Phase 501: Mathematical Correspondence Framework

## Objective
Search for independently derived mathematical systems
that share meaningful structural correspondence with
SFH-SGP organizational invariants.

## Critical Requirement
External systems must NOT be generated from SFH-SGP.
Correspondence must be pre-defined, measurable,
null-controlled, and statistically bounded.

## Eight Independent Mathematical Systems

### 1. Renormalization Group (RG)
7 operator types ordered by relevance:
- RG1: Most relevant operator (scaling dimension > 0)
- RG2: Relevant operator
- RG3: Marginal operator (dimension = 0)
- RG4: Weakly irrelevant operator
- RG5: Irrelevant operator
- RG6: Strongly irrelevant operator
- RG7: Least relevant (highest negative dimension)
Predicted: relevance hierarchy matches emergence hierarchy

## Critical Metrics

1. correspondence_similarity: Spearman rho with SFH-SGP
2. universality_transfer: fraction with rho > 0.50
3. structural_alignment: mean absolute rank deviation
4. null_separation: z-score vs random permutations
5. correspondence_consistency: std across all systems
6. false_positive_risk: P( null >= observed )

## Success/Failure

Success: >= 3/8 systems show rho > 0.50, p < 0.05
Failure: No system exceeds null at p < 0.05
