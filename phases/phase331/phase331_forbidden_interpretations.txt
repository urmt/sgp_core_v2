================================================================================

================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "recursive self-consistency proves self-awareness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive self-consistency
   corresponds to self-awareness, consciousness, or subjective experience.
   Phase 331 does not study self-awareness, consciousness, or subjective
   experience.

2. "recursive organization implies cognition"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive organization
   corresponds to cognitive processing, mental recursion, or thought.
   Phase 331 does not study cognition or mental processes.

3. "self-preservation implies observer recursion"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational self-preservation
   corresponds to observer recursion, self-reference, or identity
   persistence. Phase 331 does not study observers or identity.

4. "recursion strength implies consciousness recursion"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursion strength corresponds
   to consciousness recursion, recursive awareness, or stream of
   consciousness. Phase 331 does not study consciousness.

5. "bounded recursive drift indicates semantic self-reference"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to semantic self-reference, linguistic recursion, or self-referential
   meaning. Phase 331 does not study semantics or language.

6. "recursion structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursion structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 331 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "recursive consistency proves self-modeling"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive consistency
   corresponds to self-modeling, self-representation, or meta-cognition.
   Phase 331 does not study self-modeling or meta-cognition.
