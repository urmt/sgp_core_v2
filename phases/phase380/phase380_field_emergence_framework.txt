# Phase 380 Framework: Organizational Field Emergence Analysis

## Purpose
Determine whether recursively conserved organizational structures generate distributed field-like organizational influence across recursive topology and transport networks.

## Structure

### 1. Distributed Influence Mapping
- Map nonlocal organizational influence patterns
- Measure distributed influence index (DII)
- Identify influence propagation range

### 2. Stabilization Gradient Analysis
- Analyze stabilization gradients across topology
- Measure stabilization gradient strength (SGS)
- Identify gradient decay patterns

### 3. Field-Mediated Transport Tracking
- Track transport flows shaped by field organization
- Measure transport field alignment (TFA)
- Identify field-mediated transport pathways

### 4. Perturbation Propagation Geometry
- Map perturbation propagation through field dynamics
- Measure propagation distortion
- Identify field-mediated propagation patterns

### 5. Attractor Field Coupling
- Map coupling between attractor basins and field structures
- Measure attractor field coupling (AFC)
- Identify coupled attractor-field regimes

### 6. Recursive Field Coherence Persistence
- Track field coherence across recursive depths
- Measure recursive field persistence (RFP)
- Identify field coherence degradation patterns

### 7. Null Field Controls
- Randomized field baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- field_coherence (FC)
- distributed_influence_index (DII)
- stabilization_gradient_strength (SGS)
- recursive_field_persistence (RFP)
- transport_field_alignment (TFA)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- attractor_field_coupling (AFC)

## Classification Thresholds
- FIELD-STABLE: FC > 0.85, DII > 0.85, RFP > 0.80
- FIELD-BOUNDED: FC > 0.75, DII > 0.75, RFP > 0.70
- FIELD-PARTIAL: FC > 0.65, DII > 0.65, RFP > 0.60
- FIELD-DEGRADING: FC > 0.50
- FIELD-FAILED: FC <= 0.50
