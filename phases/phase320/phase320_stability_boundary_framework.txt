================================================================================
A. STABILITY BOUNDARY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Organizational region separating persistent recursive regimes from
degrading recursive regimes under increasing depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The coherence of the transition zone between stable and unstable
recursive behavior.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability strength measures the sharpness and coherence of the
boundary region.

================================================================================
B. ORGANIZATIONAL THRESHOLD
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Depth point at which recursive organization transitions from
persistence to degradation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The depth value where organizational metrics begin to degrade
significantly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Threshold depth marks the boundary location in recursive space.

================================================================================
C. RECURSIVE DEGRADATION REGION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Depth region beyond the organizational threshold where recursive
structure exhibits degraded properties.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The rate of degradation in the post-threshold region.

QUANTIFICATION:
Degradation rate measures the slope of organizational decline.

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED STABILITY DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of stability boundary during
recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which boundary position remains controlled
rather than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability drift measures the variance of boundary location.

================================================================================
E. RG BOUNDARY PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of stability boundary structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which boundary structure survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity measures the correlation between fine and coarse
boundary metrics.
