## What the Results DO Imply (Permitted Interpretations)

### 1. Component-Selective Shared Structure
- ✅ SFH-SGP and these mathematical systems share similar organizational principles in specific components
- ✅ The sharing is selective: different systems align with different SFH-SGP components
- ✅ Localization persists across multiple organizational domains (emergence, operators, depth, etc.)

### 2. Bounded Correspondence with Internal Structure
- ✅ Correspondence is not global but has identifiable internal structure
- ✅ Different mathematical systems map to different SFH-SGP localization patterns
- ✅ Some SFH-SGP components (like information channels) show no correspondence in tested systems

### 3. Empirical Constraint on Organization
- ✅ SFH-SGP's organizational components capture principles that appear in diverse mathematical contexts
- ✅ The localization is empirically grounded in specific, measurable organizational similarities
- ✅ Component boundaries are reproducible and measurable through statistical testing

### 4. Statistical Significance of Localization
- ✅ The localization is unlikely to arise by chance (p < 0.05 for significant components)
- ✅ Effect sizes are substantial (ρ ≥ 0.96 in multiple systems/components)
- ✅ Results withstand rigorous null hypothesis testing with permutation controls

### 5. Component-Specific Interpretive Discipline
- ✅ Only shared constrained organizational structure in specific components is implied
- ✅ No claims about fundamental reality, consciousness, or causation in any component
- ✅ Localization is descriptive, not explanatory
- ✅ Boundaries, limitations, and non-localizing components are explicitly acknowledged

## Boundary Conditions

These forbidden interpretations remain prohibited regardless of:
- Strength of localization (even if ρ = 1.0 for all components/systems)
- Statistical significance (even if p < 0.0001)
- Number of components showing localization
- Consistency of localization patterns across mathematical systems
- Independent replication by other researchers
- Different component decompositions or mathematical system representations

The Tier 3 interpretive discipline applies uniformly: localization implies ONLY shared constrained organizational structure at specific sites, nothing more.
