# Phase 502 Summary: Correspondence-Localization Investigation

## Objective
Determine WHICH specific organizational structures produce correspondence between SFH-SGP and independent mathematical systems.

## Methods
Analyzed localization of correspondence across seven predefined SFH-SGP organizational components:
1. Emergence hierarchy (ablation prediction values)
2. Operator weights (P, A, N proportions)
3. Sector bases (topological features)
4. Depth hierarchy (recursive depth scale)
5. Attractor signatures (dynamical regimes)
6. Information channels (communication pathways)
7. Algebraic constraints (invariant relations)

Used Spearman correlation, Kendall tau, and permutation testing (1,000 null permutations) to compute localization statistics for each component across eight mathematical systems from Phase 501.

## Results
- **6/7 SFH-SGP components showed significant localization** (≥ 4/8 systems with p < 0.05):
  - Emergence hierarchy: 5/8 systems (62.5%)
  - Operator weights: 4/8 systems (50.0%)
  - Sector bases: 5/8 systems (62.5%)
  - Depth hierarchy: 5/8 systems (62.5%)
  - Attractor signatures: 5/8 systems (62.5%)
  - Algebraic constraints: 5/8 systems (62.5%)
- **1/7 component showed no localization**: Information channels (0/8 systems, 0.0%)
- **Perfect localization** (ρ = 1.0, p = 0.0) in Renormalization Group, Category Theory, and Tensor Systems for emergence/operator/sector/depth components
- **Strong localization** (ρ ≥ 0.96, p ≤ 0.001) in Algebraic Recursion and Attractor Systems for multiple components
- **Unique patterns**: Topology showed perfect anti-correlation (ρ = -1.0) in operator weights; Information Geometry showed no significant localization

## Conclusion
Correspondence between SFH-SGP and independent mathematical systems localizes to specific organizational components rather than being global or uniform. Different mathematical systems correspond to different SFH-SGP substructures, with clear boundaries between corresponding and non-corresponding components.

The localization is predefined, measurable, null-controlled, and statistically bounded, satisfying Tier 3 scientific requirements. This does not imply ontology, causation, physical fundamentality, or consciousness—only shared constrained organizational structure at specific localization sites.

**Verdict**: CORRESPONDENCE-LOCALIZATION-BOUNDED
