# Phase 502: Correspondence-Localization Framework

## Objective
Determine WHICH specific organizational structures produce correspondence between SFH-SGP and independent mathematical systems.

Core scientific question:
Where exactly does correspondence occur, and where does it fail?

## Primary Hypotheses
H1: Correspondence localizes to specific invariant structures.
H2: Different mathematical systems correspond to different SFH-SGP substructures.
H3: Some SFH-SGP organizational components have no external correspondence.
H4: Correspondence density varies by recursive depth and operator composition.
H5: Localization boundaries are reproducible and measurable.

## Required Analysis
- invariant overlap mapping
- operator-level correspondence
- depth-localized similarity
- topology matching
- attractor alignment
- mismatch-region identification

## Critical Metrics
1. localized_correspondence_density: Correspondence strength per SFH-SGP component
2. invariant_overlap_score: Degree of shared invariants between systems
3. operator_alignment_index: Alignment of operator hierarchies
4. recursive_depth_similarity: Similarity across recursion depths
5. mismatch_entropy: Uncertainty in non-corresponding regions
6. structural_transfer_ratio: Fraction of SFH-SGP structure that transfers

## SFH-SGP Structural Components (from Phase 407 baseline)
Based on the SFH-SGP framework established in previous phases, we analyze:
- Emergence hierarchy (7 levels from Phase 407)
- Operator types (7 fundamental operations)
- Recursive depth layers (7 depth scales)
- Topological features (7 structural motifs)
- Attractor signatures (7 dynamical regimes)
- Information channels (7 communication pathways)
- Algebraic constraints (7 invariant relations)

## Critical Requirement
External systems must NOT be generated from SFH-SGP.
Correspondence must be pre-defined, measurable, null-controlled, and statistically bounded.

## Success/Failure Criteria
Success: >= 4/6 metrics show statistically significant localization (p < 0.05)
Failure: No metric exceeds null at p < 0.05

## Primary Danger
Global averaging masking local mismatch - we must analyze components individually.

## Interpretation Discipline
Even successful localization DOES NOT imply:
- ontology,
- causation,
- physical fundamentality,
- or consciousness.

It implies ONLY:
shared constrained organizational structure at specific localization sites.
