STRICT INTERPRETATION CONSTRAINTS
================================================================================

results. These interpretations would violate the empirical and mathematical
constraints established in prior phases.

================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "symmetry breaking proves particle generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that symmetry breaking corresponds
   to particle generation, pair production, or matter creation.
   Phase 341 does not study particles or matter.

2. "asymmetry amplification proves force emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that asymmetry amplification
   corresponds to force emergence, gauge symmetry breaking, or interaction
   generation. Phase 341 does not study forces or gauge theory.

3. "cosmological symmetry-breaking claims"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that symmetry breaking corresponds
   to cosmological phase transitions, universe formation, or big bang.
   Phase 341 does not study cosmology.

4. "trajectory divergence proves consciousness emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that trajectory divergence corresponds
   to consciousness emergence, mind differentiation, or subjective experience
   emergence. Phase 341 does not study consciousness.

5. "role stabilization proves soul or spirit"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that role stabilization corresponds
   to soul, spirit, or immaterial essence persistence. Phase 341 does not
   study souls or spirits.

6. "spontaneous differentiation proves spacetime emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that spontaneous differentiation
   corresponds to spacetime emergence, dimensional birth, or quantum gravity.
   Phase 341 does not study spacetime.

7. "symmetry-breaking geometry proves physics laws"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that symmetry-breaking geometry
   confirms physical laws, conservation principles, or fundamental physics.
   Phase 341 does not study physical laws.

8. "organizational specialization proves biological evolution"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational specialization
   corresponds to biological evolution, natural selection, or adaptation.
   Phase 341 does not study biology.

9. "differentiation proves information creation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that differentiation corresponds
   to information creation, Maxwell's demon, or entropy decrease.
   Phase 341 does not study information theory.

10. "symmetry breaking proves laws of physics derived"

    REASON: This interpretation assumes that symmetry breaking confirms
    scientific discovery, physical principle derivation, or fundamental
    truth. Phase 341 does not claim any discoveries.
