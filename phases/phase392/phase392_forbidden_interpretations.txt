# Phase 392 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Physics, physical reality, spacetime, matter, energy
- Geometry (beyond relational structure), space (beyond ordering)
- Distance, metric (beyond relational comparison), topology (beyond connectivity)
- Coordinate, dimension, manifold (beyond abstract ordering)
- Location, position, neighborhood (beyond relational proximity)
- Shape, form, curvature (beyond relational pattern)
- Causality (beyond relational dependency)
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Geometric emergence as "creating space" or "generating geometry"
- Relational metric as "measuring physical distance"
- Topology formation as "creating spatial topology"
- Spatial transport as "motion through space"
- Locality geometry as "spatial neighborhood"
- Recursive spatialization as "generating dimensions"
- Metric consistency as "preserving physical distances"
- Null geometry as "empty space"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric (as relational), topological (as connectivity)
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 392 analyzes recursive geometric emergence and relational spatialization as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All references to "geometry," "metric," "topology," "spatial," and "spatialization" are strictly mathematical and relational — describing organizational patterns in recursive relations, not physical geometry or space. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
