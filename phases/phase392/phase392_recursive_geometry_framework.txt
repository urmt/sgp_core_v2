## Purpose
Determine whether constrained proto-physical recursive systems can generate stable relational geometric organization and emergent spatial-like structure from recursive interaction constraints alone.

## Core Distinction
proto-physical propagation dynamics vs emergent recursive relational geometry

### 2. Recursive Topology Formation

- Measure topology persistence (TP)
- Identify stable topological boundaries

### 3. Relational Geometric Coherence Mapping
- Map geometric coherence patterns in recursive structure

### 4. Transport-Induced Spatial Persistence

### 5. Locality-Driven Geometric Stabilization
- Analyze geometric stabilization from locality constraints

### 6. Recursive Metric Consistency Tracking
- Track metric consistency across recursive depths

### 7. Null Geometry Controls

- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- geometric_coherence (GC)
- relational_metric_stability (RMS)
- topology_persistence (TP)
- spatial_transport_alignment (STA)
- locality_geometry_consistency (LGC)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- recursive_spatialization_gain (RSG)

## Classification Thresholds
- GEOMETRY-STABLE: GC > 0.90, RMS > 0.85, TP > 0.85
- GEOMETRY-BOUNDED: GC > 0.80, RMS > 0.75, TP > 0.75
- GEOMETRY-PARTIAL: GC > 0.70, RMS > 0.65, TP > 0.65
- GEOMETRY-DEGRADING: GC > 0.55
- GEOMETRY-FAILED: GC <= 0.55
