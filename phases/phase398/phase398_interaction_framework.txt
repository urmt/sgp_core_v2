# Phase 398 Framework: Recursive Interaction and Collision Dynamics

## Purpose
Determine whether localized recursive persistence structures naturally generate stable interaction and collision behaviors without predefined force laws or interaction primitives.

## Core Distinction
isolated localized persistence vs stable recursive interaction organization

## Structure

### 1. Packet Collision Dynamics
- Analyze collision dynamics between localized packets
- Measure interaction coherence (IC)
- Identify collision-stable regimes

### 2. Recursive Interaction Persistence Mapping
- Map persistence of interaction outcomes across recursion
- Measure collision reproducibility (CR)
- Identify interaction persistence patterns

### 3. Identity Retention After Interaction
- Track identity preservation through collision events
- Measure identity retention post-collision (IRPC)
- Identify identity-stable regimes

### 4. Exchange/Conservation Tracking
- Track exchange and conservation structures in interactions
- Measure recursive exchange stability (RES)
- Identify conservation-stable regimes

### 5. Constructive/Destructive Collision Analysis
- Analyze constructive/destructive collision outcomes
- Measure interaction gain (IG)
- Identify collision outcome regimes

### 6. Recursive Interaction Reproducibility
- Assess reproducibility of interaction transformations
- Measure bounded interaction drift (BID)
- Identify reproducible transformation regimes

### 7. Null Interaction Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm interaction signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- interaction_coherence (IC)
- collision_reproducibility (CR)
- identity_retention_post_collision (IRPC)
- recursive_exchange_stability (RES)
- bounded_interaction_drift (BID)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- interaction_gain (IG)

## Classification Thresholds
- INTERACTION-STABLE: IC > 0.90, CR > 0.85, IRPC > 0.85
- INTERACTION-BOUNDED: IC > 0.80, CR > 0.75, IRPC > 0.75
- INTERACTION-PARTIAL: IC > 0.70, CR > 0.65, IRPC > 0.65
- INTERACTION-DEGRADING: IC > 0.55
- INTERACTION-FAILED: IC <= 0.55
