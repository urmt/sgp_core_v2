## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 398 analyzes recursive interaction and collision dynamics as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All references to "collision," "interaction," "exchange," and "identity" are strictly mathematical — describing relational organizational pattern transformations, not physical particle collisions or fundamental forces. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
