# Phase 387 Framework: Self-Referential Recursive Modeling and Reflexive Organization

## Purpose
Determine whether recursive organizational systems with persistent internal representations can recursively model their own organizational structure, stabilization dynamics, and future evolution.

## Structure

### 1. Self-Model Extraction
- Extract self-referential models from organizational structure
- Measure self-reference fidelity (SRF)
- Identify self-model extraction thresholds

### 2. Reflexive Representation Fidelity
- Analyze fidelity of self-referential representations
- Measure reflexive coherence (RC)
- Identify reflexive degradation patterns

### 3. Recursive Self-Simulation Analysis
- Analyze self-simulation of future recursive states
- Measure self-simulation alignment (SSA)
- Identify simulation precision patterns

### 4. Self-Model Stabilization Gain
- Track stabilization improvement from self-modeling
- Measure reflexive stabilization gain (RSG)
- Identify stabilization thresholds

### 5. Recursive Reflexive Persistence
- Track reflexive persistence across recursive depths
- Measure recursive self-persistence (RSP)
- Identify persistence degradation patterns

### 6. Self-Referential Perturbation Response
- Track response to self-referential perturbations
- Measure self-model adaptation strength (SMAS)
- Identify adaptation dynamics

### 7. Null Self-Reference Controls
- Randomized self-reference baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- self_reference_fidelity (SRF)
- reflexive_coherence (RC)
- self_simulation_alignment (SSA)
- reflexive_stabilization_gain (RSG)
- recursive_self_persistence (RSP)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- self_model_adaptation_strength (SMAS)

## Classification Thresholds
- SELF-REF-STABLE: SRF > 0.90, RC > 0.90, SSA > 0.85
- SELF-REF-BOUNDED: SRF > 0.80, RC > 0.80, SSA > 0.75
- SELF-REF-PARTIAL: SRF > 0.70, RC > 0.70, SSA > 0.65
- SELF-REF-DEGRADING: SRF > 0.55
- SELF-REF-FAILED: SRF <= 0.55
