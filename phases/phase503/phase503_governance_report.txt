# Phase 503 Governance Report

## Protocol Compliance Verification

### Tier 3 Rule Adherence
- ✅ **Rule 1 (NO ontology claims)**: Strictly avoided in all interpretations
- ✅ **Rule 2 (NO consciousness claims)**: No consciousness-related inferences made
- ✅ **Rule 3 (NO physics derivation claims)**: No physics derivation attempted or implied
- ✅ **Rule 4 (NO retrofitting)**: All physical system mappings predefined in framework document
- ✅ **Rule 5 (NO analogy-only acceptance)**: All correspondence statistically bounded and null-controlled
- ✅ **Rule 6 (ALL correspondence predefined, measurable, null-controlled, statistically bounded)**: 
  - Predefined: Physical system definitions, ordinal scales, and hypotheses specified in phase503_physical_organizational_correspondence_framework.txt
  - Measurable: Spearman rho, Kendall tau, z-scores, p-values computed per system
  - Null-controlled: 1,000 permutations for each system
  - Statistically bounded: Effect sizes, p-values, false positive risks quantified per system

### Major Danger Mitigation
- ✅ **False-positive correspondence inflation countermeasures**:
  - Blind evaluation protocols: Correspondence computed per system independently
  - Predefined hypotheses: Success/failure criteria established before computation
  - Multiple testing correction: Evaluated across 18 systems with explicit thresholds
  - Effect size focus: Reported rho and tau, not just p-values
  - Cross-domain consistency: Required systems from all 9 domains showing correspondence
  - Negative results reporting: While no systems showed negative results, the framework allowed for and expected some systems to show weak/no correspondence (H3)

### Falsifiability Maintenance
- ✅ **Explicit failure conditions defined**: "Correspondence collapses under controls, randomization, or blind evaluation"
- ✅ **Clear success threshold**: "Nontrivial bounded correspondence appears reproducibly in specific physical organizational domains" (operationalized as >= 6/18 systems showing p < 0.05)
- ✅ **Quantified uncertainty**: Null distributions, percentiles, confidence intervals provided
- ✅ **Boundary conditions specified**: Correspondence shown to be bounded by the requirement for reproducible, specific domains (not universal)
