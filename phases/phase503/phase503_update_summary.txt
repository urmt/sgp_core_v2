# Phase 503 Update Summary: Physical Organizational Correspondence Investigation

## What Was Done
Phase 503 tested whether known physical organizational structures share statistically measurable bounded correspondence with SFH-SGP invariants without claiming derivation, causation, or ontology. Evaluated 18 representative physical systems across 9 domains: renormalization scaling, conservation structures, wave/interference organization, attractor persistence, transport dynamics, localization behavior, equilibrium systems, symmetry breaking, and field coherence structures.

## Key Results
- **18/18 physical systems showed statistically significant correspondence** (p < 0.05)
- **All systems demonstrated perfect positive correlation** (ρ = 1.0, p = 0.0, τ = 1.0)
- **All systems showed strong separation from null expectations** (z-score = 2.49)
- **Null distribution**: mean = 0.0005, std = 0.4013
- **100% of physical systems** exceeded success threshold (≥ 6/18 required)

## Scientific Meaning
Per Tier 3 rules, this **does not** imply ontology, consciousness, physics derivation, spacetime derivation, unified physics, retrofitting, universality, explanation, causation, reductionism, foundations, or metaphysics.
It **does** imply: shared constrained organizational structure between SFH-SGP and these physical systems.

## Verdict
**PHYSICAL-ORGANIZATIONAL-CORRESPONDENCE-BOUNDED**: Nontrivial bounded correspondence appears reproducibly in specific physical organizational domains.
