8. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

H1 (PERSISTENCE RENEWAL > 0.75): PASS
- P-A-N, P-A, Projection achieve self-maintaining structure

H2 (REPAIR STRENGTH > 0.70): PARTIAL
- P-A-N maintains > 0.70 through depth 20
- A-N collapses before depth 8

H3 (DEGRADATION CONTAINMENT > 0.70): PARTIAL
- P-A-N maintains > 0.70 through depth 8
- Neutral drops below at depth 2

H4 (RG SIMILARITY > 0.90): FAIL
- Only P-A-N at depth 1 achieves > 0.90
- Scale invariance limited to strong networks at early depths

H5 (SELF-MAINTENANCE HIERARCHY): PASS
- P-A-N > P-A > P-N > A-N preserved across depths

OVERALL VERDICT: WEAKLY_SELF-MAINTAINING
- Self-maintenance exists in strong networks (P-A-N, P-A)
- Repair strength maintained for P-A-N through depth 20
- Degradation containment bounded for P-A-N at early depths
- Weak systems collapse
- Scale invariance limited
