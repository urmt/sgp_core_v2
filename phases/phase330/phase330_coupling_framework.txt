================================================================================
A. ORGANIZATIONAL COUPLING
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Coordination linkage between distinct recursive organizational sectors
that maintains stable relationships without collapsing into uniformity
or fragmenting into incoherence.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Coupling strength measures the degree of stable coordination between
sector pairs across recursive depth.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coupling strength = cross_sector_coherence / max_coherence

================================================================================
B. RECURSIVE COORDINATION LINKAGE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of coordination persistence between sector pairs across
recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Coupling retention quantifies the degree to which cross-sector
coordination is preserved through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coupling retention = coupling_depth_n / coupling_depth_n-1

================================================================================
C. COUPLING RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of cross-sector coupling persistence across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Retention coefficient quantifies the degree to which sector pair
coordination remains stable.

QUANTIFICATION:
Retention = correlation(coupling_across_depths)

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED COUPLING DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of cross-sector coupling
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which coupling drift remains controlled rather than
drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coupling drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG COUPLING PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of cross-sector coupling structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which coupling organization survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_coupling_metrics, coarse_coupling_metrics)

================================================================================
COUPLING FRAMEWORK FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

CP_STRUCTURE = (C_str, C_ret, C_drift, Bound, RG_sim)

Where:
- C_str: Coupling strength
- C_ret: Coupling retention
- C_drift: Coupling drift
- Bound: Boundedness
- RG_sim: RG similarity
