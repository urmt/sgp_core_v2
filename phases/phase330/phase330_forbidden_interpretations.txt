   interactions, or bindings.

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that cross-sector coupling
   corresponds to interaction fields, force carriers, or field mediators.
   Phase 330 does not study fields, forces, or mediators.

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that coupling retention
   corresponds to observer synchronization, shared perspective, or
   common measurement frame. Phase 330 does not study observers or
   synchronization.

4. "coupling strength implies consciousness binding"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that coupling strength corresponds
   to consciousness binding, unified experience, or awareness integration.
   Phase 330 does not study consciousness or subjective experience.

5. "bounded coupling drift indicates physical field dynamics"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to physical field dynamics, energy transfer, or field propagation.
   Phase 330 does not study physical fields or dynamics.

6. "coupling structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that coupling structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 330 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "cross-sector coordination proves semantic synchronization"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that cross-sector coordination
   corresponds to semantic synchronization, meaning alignment, or
   linguistic coupling. Phase 330 does not study semantics or language.
