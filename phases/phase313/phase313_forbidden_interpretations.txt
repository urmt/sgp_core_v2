STRICT INTERPRETATION CONSTRAINTS
================================================================================

This document explicitly prohibits certain interpretations of Phase 313
results. These interpretations would violate the empirical and mathematical
constraints established in prior phases.

================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "adaptive structure proves intelligence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that adaptive reconfiguration implies
   intelligent behavior. Phase 313 does not study intelligence, cognition,
   or mental processes. We study organizational restructuring within a
   mathematical framework.

2. "recursive adaptation proves learning"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive adaptive reconfiguration
   corresponds to learning processes. Phase 313 does not study learning,
   memory, or knowledge acquisition. We study persistence and stability
   within a mathematical model.

3. "stable reconfiguration proves optimization"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable reconfiguration organization
   implies optimization processes. Phase 313 does not address questions of
   optimization, fitness, or utility maximization. We study organizational
   reconfiguration within empirical constraints.

4. "RG adaptation proves cognition"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   adaptive stability implies cognitive processes. Phase 313 does not study
   cognition, awareness, or mental states. We study scale invariance within
   a mathematical framework.

5. "organizational adaptation is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that adaptive reconfiguration
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 313 does not study
   universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study
   adaptive reconfiguration within empirical constraints.

6. "adaptive strength implies biological evolution"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that adaptive metrics correspond
   to biological fitness or evolutionary processes. Phase 313 does not
   study evolution, natural selection, or biological systems. We study
   organizational structure within a mathematical model.

7. "reconfiguration stability implies goal-directed behavior"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that reconfiguration stability
   corresponds to goal-seeking or purposive behavior. Phase 313 does not
   study goals, purposes, or intentional behavior. We study structural
   persistence under recursive reconfiguration.
