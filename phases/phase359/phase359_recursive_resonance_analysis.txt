- Scale invariance limited to strong networks at early depths

- P-A-N > P-A > P-N > A-N preserved across depths

OVERALL VERDICT: WEAKLY_RESONANT
- Resonant stability exists in strong networks (P-A-N, P-A)
- Cooperative enhancement maintained for P-A-N through depth 20
- Resonance spread bounded for P-A-N
- Weak systems collapse
- Scale invariance limited
