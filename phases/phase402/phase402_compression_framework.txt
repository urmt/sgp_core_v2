# Phase 402 Framework: Compression-Generalization Validation

## Purpose
Determine whether the recursive emergence hierarchy survives substantial compression, simplification, parameter reduction, and generalized representation constraints. Distinguish overfit recursive construction from compressible structural emergence.

## Core Distinction
overfit recursive construction vs compressible structural emergence

## Compression Conditions
1. FullBaselineArchitecture — no compression
2. ReducedSectorCount — blurred sector differentiation
3. CompressedOperatorBasis — quantized operator representation
4. LowPrecisionRecursion — low-precision emergence values
5. SparseRecursiveConnectivity — fractional connection retention
6. CompressedMemoryRepresentation — reduced recursive memory depth
7. ReducedInteractionChannels — fewer interaction pathways
8. SimplifiedTransportDynamics — coarser transport gradients
9. CoarseGrainedRecursiveDepth — compressed depth resolution
10. MinimalSufficientArchitecture — simplest viable structure
11. RandomizedParameterCompression — compression with random dropout
12. GeneralizedOperatorAbstraction — approximated operators
13. RecursiveDimensionalReduction — reduced emergent dimensions
14. ConstrainedPropagationBandwidth — bandwidth-limited propagation
15. NullCompressedRecursionControl — complete collapse

## Metrics
- compression_survival_fraction (CSF) — emergence surviving compression
- emergence_reconstruction_fidelity (ERF) — fidelity of compressed emergence
- minimal_architecture_score (MAS) — score under minimal architecture
- generalization_consistency (GC) — variance across compression types
- recursive_information_efficiency (RIE) — emergence per parameter ratio
- compression_degradation_bound (CDB) — max degradation under compression
- structural_redundancy_index (SRI) — sector-level redundancy

## Classification Thresholds
- COMPRESSION-STABLE: CSF > 0.80, ERF > 0.80
- COMPRESSION-BOUNDED: CSF > 0.60, ERF > 0.60
- COMPRESSION-DEGRADING: CSF > 0.40, ERF > 0.40
- COMPRESSION-FAILED: CSF <= 0.40
