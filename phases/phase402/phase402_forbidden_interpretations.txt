PHASE 402 FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS

The following interpretations of compression-generalization results
are FORBIDDEN. These terms reify data compression, machine learning
generalization, or information theory metaphors as literal properties.
All results must be framed as relational propagation metrics under
architectural simplification.

FORBIDDEN TERMS AND PHRASES

DO NOT claim:
1. "Lossy compression" in data compression sense
2. "JPEG compression" or "MP3 compression"
3. "Codec" or "encoding scheme"
4. "Bit rate" or "bit depth" in digital sense
5. "Sampling rate" or "Nyquist frequency"
6. "Machine learning generalization"
7. "Train/test split" or "train/test generalization"
8. "Overfitting" or "underfitting"
9. "Regularization" or "L1/L2 regularization"
10. "Dropout" (neural network training)
11. "Neural network pruning"
12. "Model distillation" or "knowledge distillation"
13. "Quantization-aware training"
14. "Principal Component Analysis" (as literal PCA)
15. "Autoencoder" or "variational autoencoder"
16. "Latent space" or "latent representation"
17. "Manifold learning" or "manifold hypothesis"
18. "Information bottleneck"
19. "Rate-distortion theory"
20. "Minimum description length"
21. "Kolmogorov complexity" (as literal computation)
22. "Algorithmic information theory"
23. "Minimum necessary information"
24. "Compressed sensing"
25. "Sparse coding" or "sparse representation"
26. "Wavelet compression" or "Fourier compression"
27. "Fractal compression"
28. "Entropy coding" or "Huffman coding"
29. "Dictionary learning"
30. "Lossless vs lossy" as digital compression

PERMITTED ALTERNATIVES

Instead of the above, use:
- "Compression survival fraction" (CSF)
- "Architectural simplification tolerance"
- "Emergence reconstruction fidelity"
- "Structural redundancy quantification"
- "Minimal viable architecture"
- "Recursive information efficiency"
- "Compression-degradation threshold"
- "Parameter reduction survival"
- "Sector differentiation redundancy"
- "Operator precision tolerance"

RATIONALIZATION

Phase 402 investigates whether the emergence hierarchy survives
simplification. The compressions examined (sector blur, quantization,
sparsity, memory reduction, dimensional reduction) are mathematical
simplifications of recursive constraint propagation — not digital
compression algorithms, machine learning regularization, or
information-theoretic coding. All terminology must remain strictly
relational and mathematical.

Enforcement: Any use of forbidden terms in phase documentation or
derived publications requires reframing. Repeat violations may
require governance review.
