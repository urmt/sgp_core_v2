# Phase 381 Framework: Field-Interference and Coherent Superposition Analysis

## Purpose
Determine whether distributed organizational fields can interact through stable interference and coherent superposition without destroying recursive organizational integrity.

## Structure

### 1. Field Interference Mapping
- Map interference patterns between organizational fields
- Measure interference coherence (IC)
- Identify constructive vs. destructive interference regions

### 2. Coherent Superposition Analysis
- Analyze superposition of multiple field structures
- Measure superposition stability (SS)
- Identify coherent superposition regimes

### 3. Constructive/Destructive Interaction Tracking
- Track constructive and destructive interaction dynamics
- Measure constructive interaction gain (CIG)
- Measure destructive interference bound (DIB)

### 4. Attractor Synchronization Dynamics
- Track attractor field synchronization through interaction
- Measure recursive field synchronization (RFS)
- Identify synchronization thresholds

### 5. Emergent Interference Regime Detection
- Detect emergent organizational regimes from interference
- Measure emergent regime stability (ERS)
- Identify regime transition patterns

### 6. Recursive Interference Persistence
- Track interference patterns across recursive depths
- Measure interference persistence
- Identify interference degradation patterns

### 7. Null Interference Controls
- Randomized interference baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- interference_coherence (IC)
- superposition_stability (SS)
- constructive_interaction_gain (CIG)
- destructive_interference_bound (DIB)
- recursive_field_synchronization (RFS)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- emergent_regime_stability (ERS)

## Classification Thresholds
- INTERFERENCE-COHERENT: IC > 0.85, SS > 0.85, RFS > 0.80
- INTERFERENCE-BOUNDED: IC > 0.75, SS > 0.75, RFS > 0.70
- INTERFERENCE-PARTIAL: IC > 0.65, SS > 0.65, RFS > 0.60
- INTERFERENCE-DEGRADING: IC > 0.50
- INTERFERENCE-FAILED: IC <= 0.50
