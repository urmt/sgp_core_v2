# Phase 386 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Learning, adaptation, memory, recognition
- Meaning, significance, interpretation, representation
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)
- Evolution, emergence (beyond structural pattern description)
- Intelligence, wisdom, knowledge
- Identity, self, personality, character

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Internal models as "mental models" or "cognitive maps"
- Representation as "understanding" or "comprehension"
- Simulation as "imagination" or "visualization"
- Predictive model gain as "learning" or "improvement"
- Model-driven stabilization as "strategic planning"
- Internal representation as "conscious awareness" or "subjective experience"
- Recursive model generation as "self-reflection" or "metacognition"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric, topological, algebraic
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 386 analyzes recursive model-generation and internal representation as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
