# Phase 386 Framework: Recursive Model-Generation and Internal Representation Analysis

## Purpose
Determine whether predictive recursive organizational systems can generate stable internal organizational representations that model external recursive dynamics and future perturbations.

## Structure

### 1. Internal Model Extraction
- Extract internal organizational models from structure
- Measure internal model coherence (IMC)
- Identify model extraction thresholds

### 2. Representation Fidelity Analysis
- Analyze fidelity of internal representations
- Measure representation fidelity (RF)
- Identify fidelity degradation patterns

### 3. Simulated Perturbation Evaluation
- Evaluate simulated vs. direct perturbation responses
- Measure simulation alignment (SA)
- Identify simulation precision patterns

### 4. Predictive Model Adaptation Tracking
- Track adaptive improvement of internal models
- Measure predictive model gain (PMG)
- Identify adaptation dynamics

### 5. Recursive Simulation Persistence
- Track simulation persistence across recursive depths
- Measure recursive representation persistence (RRP)
- Identify persistence degradation patterns

### 6. Model-Driven Stabilization Testing
- Test stabilization driven by internal models
- Measure model adaptation strength (MAS)
- Identify model-driven stabilization thresholds

### 7. Null Representation Controls
- Randomized representation baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- representation_fidelity (RF)
- internal_model_coherence (IMC)
- simulation_alignment (SA)
- predictive_model_gain (PMG)
- recursive_representation_persistence (RRP)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- model_adaptation_strength (MAS)

## Classification Thresholds
- MODEL-STABLE: RF > 0.90, IMC > 0.90, SA > 0.85
- MODEL-BOUNDED: RF > 0.80, IMC > 0.80, SA > 0.75
- MODEL-PARTIAL: RF > 0.70, IMC > 0.70, SA > 0.65
- MODEL-DEGRADING: RF > 0.55
- MODEL-FAILED: RF <= 0.55
