================================================================================
A. RECURSIVE PROPAGATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistent recursive transmission of organizational structure across
relational geometry.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which organizational patterns propagate through recursive
geometry while maintaining structure.

QUANTIFICATION:
Propagation strength measures the persistence of organizational patterns
during recursive transmission.

================================================================================
B. PROPAGATION STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Retention of bounded propagation organization under recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which propagation pathways maintain structure through
recursive transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Pathway stability measures the preservation of propagation organization.

================================================================================
C. PROPAGATION RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of identifiable propagation structure across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which propagation patterns remain identifiable as
recursion increases.

QUANTIFICATION:
Recursive retention measures the persistence of propagation identity.

================================================================================
D. PROPAGATION PATHWAY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Stable recursive organizational route across relational structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which recursive geometry supports identifiable propagation
routes between organizational structures.

QUANTIFICATION:
Pathway stability measures the coherence of propagation routes.

================================================================================
E. PROPAGATION BOUNDEDNESS
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded recursive dispersion during propagation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which propagation remains contained rather than dispersing
infinitely through recursive geometry.

QUANTIFICATION:
Boundedness measures containment of propagation structure.
Dispersion measures unbounded spread during propagation.

================================================================================
F. PROPAGATION RG STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of recursive propagation organization.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which propagation structure survives coarse-graining.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG stability measures the correlation between fine and coarse propagation metrics.
