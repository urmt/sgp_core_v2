================================================================================
A. ORGANIZATIONAL DENSITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational concentration within recursive structure,
referring to how tightly organizational properties are packed within
a given recursive region.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Density strength measures the degree of organizational concentration
across recursive depth.

QUANTIFICATION:
Density strength = organizational_concentration / max_concentration

================================================================================
B. DENSITY RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational density persistence across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Density retention quantifies the degree to which organizational density
is preserved through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Density retention = density_depth_n / density_depth_n-1

================================================================================
C. DENSITY DISPERSION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational density variance across recursive structure,
indicating whether density becomes more concentrated, distributed,
or remains balanced.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Density dispersion quantifies the degree of density variance
across depth iterations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Dispersion = variance(density_across_depths) / max_variance

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED DENSITY DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of organizational density
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which density drift remains controlled rather than
drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Density drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG DENSITY PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of organizational density structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which density organization survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_density_metrics, coarse_density_metrics)
