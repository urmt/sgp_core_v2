================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "organizational density proves physical mass density"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational density
   corresponds to physical mass density, matter concentration, or
   physical density. Phase 332 does not study mass, matter, or physical
   density.

2. "density concentration implies energy concentration"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that density concentration
   corresponds to energy concentration, energy density, or thermodynamic
   properties. Phase 332 does not study energy or thermodynamics.

3. "density persistence implies consciousness density"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that density persistence corresponds
   to consciousness density, information density in mind, or subjective
   intensity. Phase 332 does not study consciousness or subjective
   experience.

4. "bounded density drift indicates matter emergence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded drift corresponds
   to matter emergence, physical substance formation, or material
   condensation. Phase 332 does not study matter or material formation.

5. "density dispersion implies semantic compression"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that density dispersion corresponds
   to semantic compression, meaning density, or linguistic information
   concentration. Phase 332 does not study semantics or language.

6. "density structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that density structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 332 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "organizational density proves information theory"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational density
   corresponds to information density, bit density, or Shannon entropy.
   Phase 332 does not study information theory or entropy.
