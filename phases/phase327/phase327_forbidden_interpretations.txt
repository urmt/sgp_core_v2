   REASON: This interpretation assumes that curvature strength corresponds

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that deformation retention corresponds

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded curvature drift
   corresponds to gravitational light bending, geodesic deviation, or
   gravitational effects. Phase 327 does not study gravity or light.

5. "curvature stability implies observer or consciousness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that curvature stability corresponds
   to observer stability, consciousness persistence, or awareness.
   Phase 327 does not study observers or consciousness.

6. "curvature structure implies idealism or physical reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that curvature structure implies
   idealist philosophy or confirms physical reality. Phase 327 does not
   study idealism, physical reality, or philosophical positions.

7. "organizational deformation implies physical field theory"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that organizational deformation
   corresponds to field theory, energy-momentum tensors, or field dynamics.
   Phase 327 does not study fields, energy, or field theory.
