================================================================================
A. ORGANIZATIONAL CURVATURE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational deformation from flat organizational baseline
under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Curvature strength measures the degree of bounded organizational
deformation across recursive depth.

QUANTIFICATION:
Curvature strength = deviation_from_flat / max_deviation

================================================================================
B. RECURSIVE DEFORMATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Change in organizational structure resulting from recursive
transformation at each depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Deformation retention quantifies the degree to which organizational
curvature is preserved through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Deformation retention = curvature_depth_n / curvature_depth_n-1

================================================================================
C. CURVATURE STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of organizational curvature persistence across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Curvature stability coefficient quantifies the degree to which
organizational deformation remains bounded.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability = 1 - variance(curvature_across_depths) / max_variance

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED CURVATURE DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of organizational curvature
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which curvature drift remains controlled rather
than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Curvature drift boundedness = variance of drift / threshold

================================================================================
E. RG CURVATURE PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of organizational curvature structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which curvature organization survives coarse-graining
operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity = correlation(fine_curvature_metrics, coarse_curvature_metrics)

================================================================================
CURVATURE STABILITY FRAMEWORK FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

CV_STRUCTURE = (C_str, D_ret, C_drift, Bound, RG_sim)

Where:
- C_str: Curvature strength
- D_ret: Deformation retention
- C_drift: Curvature drift
- Bound: Boundedness
- RG_sim: RG similarity
