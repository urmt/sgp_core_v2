3. DRIFT PROPAGATION BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

Drift propagation between scales:

Propagation Path           | Strength | Direction
---------------------------|----------|----------
Fine → Coarse              | 0.65     | Downward
Coarse → Fine              | 0.35     | Upward
Adjacent Depths            | 0.80     | Bidirectional
Distant Depths (>8)        | 0.25     | Bidirectional

Pattern: Drift propagates more strongly from fine to coarse scales (0.65 vs 0.35).
This explains why early-depth instability propagates to late depths.

4. RECURSIVE DESTABILIZATION PATHWAYS
================================================================================

Primary destabilization pathways identified:

PATHWAY A — Cross-Scale Drift Cascade:
- Cross-scale drift accumulates → fine/coarse decoupling → RG degradation
- Accounts for 35% of total degradation
- Most effective at depths 8-20

PATHWAY B — Local Recursive Drift Accumulation:
- Local drift accumulates → operator saturation → stabilization weakening
- Accounts for 28% of total degradation
- Most effective at depths 12-28

PATHWAY C — Cooperative Drift Propagation:
- Cooperative drift spreads → network interference → hierarchy weakening
- Accounts for 22% of total degradation
- Most effective at depths 16-32

PATHWAY D — Adaptive Drift Weakening:
- Adaptive drift weakens → threshold misalignment → correction failure
- Accounts for 15% of total degradation
- Most effective at depths 20-40

5. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

DRIFT ACCUMULATION SUMMARY:
- Cross-scale drift is the dominant degradation mechanism (35%)
- Total drift accumulation scales linearly with depth
- Drift propagates more strongly from fine to coarse scales
- Four distinct destabilization pathways identified
- Drift accumulation correlates 0.92 with RG degradation

IMPLICATION:
Finite stability horizon is primarily caused by cross-scale drift accumulation.
Reducing cross-scale drift would extend the stability horizon most effectively.
