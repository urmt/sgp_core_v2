5. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

OPERATOR SATURATION SUMMARY:
- Adaptive Scale operators saturate fastest (26% at depth 40)
- Cooperative Closure operators saturate slightly slower (25% at depth 40)
- Reinforcement exhaustion follows linear decline pattern
- Operators plateau at depths 32-40 with 50-56% effectiveness loss
- Operator saturation contributes ~20% to total RG degradation

IMPLICATION:
Operator saturation is a secondary degradation mechanism.
Cross-scale drift (35%) is the primary mechanism.
Operator saturation becomes significant beyond depth 20.
