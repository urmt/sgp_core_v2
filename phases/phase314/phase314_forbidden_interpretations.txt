FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "continuity proves selfhood"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that structural continuity implies
   psychological selfhood or personal identity. Phase 314 does not study
   selfhood, ego, or personal identity. We study organizational continuity
   within a mathematical framework.

2. "recursive continuity proves identity"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive structural continuity
   corresponds to psychological or philosophical identity. Phase 314 does
   not study identity, self-reference, or personal continuity. We study
   persistence and stability within a mathematical model.

3. "stable continuity proves observers"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable continuity organization
   implies observer existence or observer invariance. Phase 314 does not
   address questions of observers, witnessing, or perceptual identity.
   We study organizational continuity within empirical constraints.

4. "RG continuity proves consciousness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   continuity stability implies consciousness or awareness. Phase 314 does
   not study consciousness, awareness, or subjective experience. We study
   scale invariance within a mathematical framework.

5. "organizational continuity is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that structural continuity
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 314 does not study
   universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study
   continuity preservation within empirical constraints.

6. "continuity strength implies psychological identity"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that continuity metrics correspond
   to psychological identity or self-concept. Phase 314 does not study
   identity concepts, self-images, or psychological structures. We study
   organizational structure within a mathematical model.

7. "structural retention implies memory"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that structural retention
   corresponds to memory or past experience preservation. Phase 314 does
   not study memory, recall, or experiential persistence. We study
   structural persistence under recursive reconfiguration.
