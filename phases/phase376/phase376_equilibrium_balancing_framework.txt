# Phase 376 Framework: Recursive Flow Equilibrium and Transport Balancing

## Purpose
Determine whether persistent recursive circulation systems can self-balance transport dynamics into stable long-horizon equilibrium regimes.

## Structure

### 1. Equilibrium Convergence Analysis
- Track convergence toward equilibrium states
- Measure equilibrium stability (ES)
- Identify equilibrium convergence thresholds

### 2. Adaptive Bottleneck Balancing
- Track adaptive bottleneck equalization
- Measure bottleneck equalization (BE)
- Identify bottleneck self-regulation patterns

### 3. Recursive Flow Redistribution
- Track flow redistribution across topology
- Measure recursive flow coherence (RFC)
- Identify redistribution stability patterns

### 4. Source/Sink Self-Correction
- Track source/sink imbalance correction
- Measure transport balance index (TBI)
- Identify self-correction dynamics

### 5. Transport Load Equalization
- Track load distribution across transport pathways
- Measure load equalization efficiency
- Identify load balancing patterns

### 6. Perturbation Equilibrium Recovery
- Apply perturbations to disrupt equilibrium
- Measure equilibrium recovery trajectories
- Identify equilibrium resilience

### 7. Null Equilibrium Controls
- Randomized equilibrium baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- equilibrium_stability (ES)
- transport_balance_index (TBI)
- bottleneck_equalization (BE)
- recursive_flow_coherence (RFC)
- asymptotic_decay_suppression (ADS)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- equilibrium_persistence (EP)

## Classification Thresholds
- EQUILIBRIUM-STABLE: ES > 0.75, TBI > 0.75, RFC > 0.70
- EQUILIBRIUM-BOUNDED: ES > 0.65, TBI > 0.65, RFC > 0.60
- EQUILIBRIUM-PARTIAL: ES > 0.55, TBI > 0.55, RFC > 0.50
- EQUILIBRIUM-DEGRADING: ES > 0.40
- EQUILIBRIUM-FAILED: ES <= 0.40
