CONFIRMATION: NO ONTOLOGICAL INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

Phase 343 does NOT introduce ontology. The reconstruction framework studies:
- Reconstruction as recovered organization after disruption
- Identity recovery as restoration of prior structure
- Recovery drift as residual degradation
- Compatibility restoration as restored coexistence
- Recursive recovery persistence as stability of recovered structure

No ontological claims are made about:
- Self-healing matter or material regeneration
- Biological healing or organismal regeneration
- Consciousness identity or self-awareness
- Quantum state restoration or error correction
- Particle interaction or force mediation
