   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded recovery drift

8. "reconstruction proves life or consciousness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that reconstruction corresponds

   Phase 343 does not study life or consciousness.

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that collapsing pairs correspond
