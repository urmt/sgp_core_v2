FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "composition proves matter"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that compositional organization
   implies physical matter. Phase 309 does not study matter formation,
   particle assembly, or material construction. We study organizational
   structure within a mathematical framework.

2. "recursive assembly proves physics"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive composition
   corresponds to physical assembly processes. Phase 309 does not study
   physical systems. We study persistence and stability within a
   mathematical model.

3. "stable composition proves reality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable compositional
   organization implies ontological reality. Phase 309 does not address
   questions of existence, reality, or being. We study organizational
   stability within empirical constraints.

4. "RG composition proves spacetime"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   stability implies the existence of physical spacetime. Phase 309
   does not study spacetime geometry, physical dimensions, or
   space-time structure. We study scale invariance within a
   mathematical framework.

5. "organizational composition is universe formation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that compositional organization
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 309 does not
   study universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis.
   We study compositional stability within empirical constraints.

6. "composition strength implies particle interactions"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that compositional metrics
   correspond to particle physics. Phase 309 does not study particles,
   interactions, or quantum field theory. We study organizational
   structure within a mathematical model.

7. "decomposition stability implies physical decay"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that decomposition stability
   corresponds to physical decay processes. Phase 309 does not study
   physical decay, material transformation, or chemical processes.
   We study identity preservation under decomposition operations.
