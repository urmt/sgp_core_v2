# Phase 377 Framework: Recursive Equilibrium Resilience and Shock Absorption

## Purpose
Determine whether recursive transport equilibrium systems can absorb, redistribute, and recover from large-scale perturbations without losing global organizational coherence.

## Structure

### 1. Large-Scale Perturbation Injection
- Inject structured perturbations into equilibrium systems
- Measure shock absorption index (SAI)
- Identify shock magnitude thresholds

### 2. Cascade Propagation Analysis
- Track cascade propagation pathways
- Measure cascade suppression (CS)
- Identify cascade containment patterns

### 3. Equilibrium Recovery Tracking
- Track recovery trajectories post-perturbation
- Measure recovery coherence (RC)
- Identify recovery time scales

### 4. Adaptive Redistribution Response
- Track adaptive transport redistribution
- Measure redistribution efficiency (RE)
- Identify redistribution pathways

### 5. Shock Absorption Basin Mapping
- Map basins that absorb shocks effectively
- Identify shock-absorbing attractor regions
- Measure basin absorption capacity

### 6. Recursive Self-Healing Dynamics
- Track self-healing trajectories
- Measure self-healing persistence (SHP)
- Identify healing rate patterns

### 7. Null Resilience Controls
- Randomized resilience baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- shock_absorption_index (SAI)
- cascade_suppression (CS)
- recovery_coherence (RC)
- redistribution_efficiency (RE)
- equilibrium_resilience (ER)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- self_healing_persistence (SHP)

## Classification Thresholds
- RESILIENCE-STABLE: SAI > 0.75, CS > 0.75, RE > 0.70
- RESILIENCE-BOUNDED: SAI > 0.65, CS > 0.65, RE > 0.60
- RESILIENCE-PARTIAL: SAI > 0.55, CS > 0.55, RE > 0.50
- RESILIENCE-DEGRADING: SAI > 0.40
- RESILIENCE-FAILED: SAI <= 0.40
