================================================================================
A. TOPOLOGICAL PROTECTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Property of recursively organized structure whereby organizational
identity persists under bounded perturbation and recursive deformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Protection strength quantifies the degree to which recursive structure
resists identity loss under perturbation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Protection = identity_preservation / max_preservation

================================================================================
B. DEFORMATION DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Structural deviation from baseline organizational configuration
under recursive perturbation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Deformation drift measures the magnitude of organizational displacement
accumulated through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Deformation = structural_deviation / baseline_scale

================================================================================
C. CLASS PRESERVATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of whether organizational identity survives recursive evolution
without transitioning to a different organizational class.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Class preservation quantifies the fraction of organizational structures
maintaining original classification through transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Preservation = preserved_classes / total_classes

================================================================================
D. PERTURBATION STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of recursive structure response to bounded organizational
disturbance.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Perturbation stability quantifies the degree to which protected
structures maintain integrity under disturbance.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability = 1 - (perturbation_response / max_response)

================================================================================
E. RECURSIVE TOPOLOGY PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of topological protection under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Topology persistence measures the degree to which protected organizational
structure survives coarse-graining.

QUANTIFICATION:
Persistence = correlation(fine_topology, coarse_topology)
