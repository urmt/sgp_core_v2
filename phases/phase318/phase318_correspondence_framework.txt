================================================================================
A. CORRESPONDENCE CONSISTENCY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
The degree to which organizational sectors maintain structurally
compatible relationships across recursive transformations.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The consistency of relational correspondence between sector pairs
under recursive depth evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Correspondence strength measures the compatibility between sector pairs.

================================================================================
B. RELATIONAL COMPATIBILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
The preservation of structural compatibility between sectors across
recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which the relative relationship between sectors remains
constant under depth transformations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Compatibility measures the stability of inter-sector relationships.

================================================================================
C. RECURSIVE CORRESPONDENCE STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Retention of correspondence behavior across recursive transformations.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which the correspondence relationship remains stable
as recursive depth increases.

QUANTIFICATION:
Correspondence stability measures the consistency of pair behavior.

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED RELATIONAL DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of sector relationships during
recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which correspondence introduces controlled variance
rather than unbounded relational drift.

QUANTIFICATION:
Relational drift measures the accumulation of correspondence variance.

================================================================================
E. RG CORRESPONDENCE PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of correspondence relationships.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which correspondence survives coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG similarity measures the correlation between fine and coarse
correspondence metrics.
