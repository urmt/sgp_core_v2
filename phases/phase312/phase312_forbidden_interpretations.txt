================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that relational equilibrium implies

   equilibrium within a mathematical framework.

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive equilibrium
   corresponds to physical processes. Phase 312 does not study physical
   systems. We study persistence and stability within a mathematical model.

3. "stable equilibrium proves matter"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable equilibrium organization
   implies material persistence. Phase 312 does not address questions of
   matter, substance, or material stability. We study organizational
   equilibrium within empirical constraints.

4. "RG equilibrium proves spacetime"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   equilibrium stability implies the existence of physical spacetime.
   Phase 312 does not study spacetime geometry, physical dimensions, or
   space-time structure. We study scale invariance within a mathematical
   framework.

5. "organizational equilibrium is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that relational equilibrium
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 312 does not study
   universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study
   equilibrium stabilization within empirical constraints.

6. "equilibrium strength implies energy minimization"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that equilibrium metrics correspond
   to energy minimization principles. Phase 312 does not study energy,
   potentials, or minimization principles. We study organizational
   structure within a mathematical model.

7. "stabilization implies physical relaxation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive stabilization
   corresponds to physical relaxation processes. Phase 312 does not study
   relaxation, decay, or physical processes. We study structural
   stabilization under recursive propagation.
