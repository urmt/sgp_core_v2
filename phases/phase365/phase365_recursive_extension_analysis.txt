6. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

RECURSIVE EXTENSION SUMMARY:
- Closure does not persist above 0.70 beyond depth 20
- RG similarity remains above 0.48 through depth 32 (gradual degradation)
- Degradation rate stabilizes at -0.010 per depth unit from depths 16-32
- Strong networks maintain RG > 0.55 through depth 24
- Weak networks drop below 0.55 at depths 16-20
- Relative degradation is consistent across all depths

IMPLICATION:
RG closure is a shallow-depth phenomenon (depths 1-8).
Beyond depth 20, closure degrades gradually but does not collapse abruptly.
This suggests closure mechanisms are effective at early depths but
cannot fully counteract depth-dependent degradation at late depths.
