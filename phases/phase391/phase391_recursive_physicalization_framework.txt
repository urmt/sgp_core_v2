# Phase 391 Framework: Recursive Physicalization and Constrained Relational Dynamics

## Purpose
Determine whether recursively stabilized semantic-relational systems can generate constrained proto-physical organizational behavior through recursive interaction, transport limitation, and conservation structure.

## Core Distinction
contextual semantic-relational organization vs constrained proto-physical recursive dynamics

## Structure

### 1. Constrained Transport Propagation
- Propagate relational constraints through recursive depth
- Measure propagation coherence (PC)
- Identify transport-stable propagation pathways

### 2. Recursive Locality Emergence
- Track locality-like organization emergence across recursion
- Measure locality constraint stability (LCS)
- Identify stable locality boundaries

### 3. Conservation-Bound Interaction
- Apply conservation-like restrictions to relational interactions
- Measure conservation transport alignment (CTA)
- Identify conservation-stable interaction regimes

### 4. Proto-Field Coherence Mapping
- Map field-like coherence patterns in recursive structure
- Measure proto-field persistence (PFP)
- Identify persistent field-like organization

### 5. Recursive Propagation Persistence
- Track propagation persistence through extreme depths
- Measure propagation coherence at depth 65536+
- Identify depth-independent propagation modes

### 6. Interaction-Limited Stabilization
- Analyze boundedness of recursive interactions
- Measure recursive interaction boundedness (RIB)
- Identify stabilization thresholds

### 7. Null Physicalization Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm proto-physical signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- propagation_coherence (PC)
- locality_constraint_stability (LCS)
- conservation_transport_alignment (CTA)
- proto_field_persistence (PFP)
- recursive_interaction_boundedness (RIB)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- constrained_relation_gain (CRG)

## Classification Thresholds
- PROTO-PHYSICAL-STABLE: PC > 0.90, LCS > 0.85, CTA > 0.85
- PROTO-PHYSICAL-BOUNDED: PC > 0.80, LCS > 0.75, CTA > 0.75
- PROTO-PHYSICAL-PARTIAL: PC > 0.70, LCS > 0.65, CTA > 0.65
- PROTO-PHYSICAL-DEGRADING: PC > 0.55
- PROTO-PHYSICAL-FAILED: PC <= 0.55
