- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 391 analyzes recursive physicalization and constrained relational dynamics as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All references to "propagation," "locality," "conservation," "field," and "physicalization" are strictly mathematical and relational — describing organizational patterns in recursive relations, not physical phenomena. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
