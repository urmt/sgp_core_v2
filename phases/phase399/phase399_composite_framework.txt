# Phase 399 Framework: Recursive Composite Binding and Structural Formation

## Purpose
Determine whether interacting recursive localized persistence structures naturally generate stable composite bound organizational regimes without predefined binding forces or assembly primitives.

## Core Distinction
transient interaction dynamics vs persistent recursively bound composite organization

## Structure

### 1. Composite Binding Emergence
- Detect emergence of composite bound organizational states
- Measure composite coherence (CC)
- Identify binding-stable regimes

### 2. Recursive Bound-State Persistence Tracking
- Track persistence of bound composite states across recursion
- Measure binding persistence (BP)
- Identify bound-state degradation patterns

### 3. Internal Coherence Stability Analysis
- Analyze internal coherence maintenance within composites
- Measure recursive internal stability (RIS)
- Identify internal coherence regimes

### 4. Perturbation Resilience of Composite Structures
- Assess composite resilience under perturbation
- Measure bounded composite drift (BCD)
- Identify resilient binding regimes

### 5. Recursive Assembly Hierarchy Mapping
- Map hierarchical assembly structures
- Measure assembly reproducibility (AR)
- Identify hierarchical assembly regimes

### 6. Composite Transport Stability
- Track transport stability of composite structures
- Measure composite gain (CG)
- Identify transport-stable regimes

### 7. Null Binding Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm composite signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- composite_coherence (CC)
- binding_persistence (BP)
- recursive_internal_stability (RIS)
- assembly_reproducibility (AR)
- bounded_composite_drift (BCD)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- composite_gain (CG)

## Classification Thresholds
- COMPOSITE-STABLE: CC > 0.90, BP > 0.85, RIS > 0.85
- COMPOSITE-BOUNDED: CC > 0.80, BP > 0.75, RIS > 0.75
- COMPOSITE-PARTIAL: CC > 0.70, BP > 0.65, RIS > 0.65
- COMPOSITE-DEGRADING: CC > 0.55
- COMPOSITE-FAILED: CC <= 0.55
