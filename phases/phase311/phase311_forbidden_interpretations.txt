================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that constraint propagation implies

   constraint propagation within a mathematical framework.

2. "recursive propagation proves physics"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive constraint propagation
   corresponds to physical processes. Phase 311 does not study physical
   systems. We study persistence and stability within a mathematical model.

3. "stable propagation proves interactions"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable propagation organization
   implies physical interactions. Phase 311 does not address questions of
   interaction, coupling, or force mediation. We study organizational
   propagation within empirical constraints.

4. "RG propagation proves spacetime"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   propagation stability implies the existence of physical spacetime.
   Phase 311 does not study spacetime geometry, physical dimensions, or
   space-time structure. We study scale invariance within a mathematical
   framework.

5. "organizational propagation is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that constraint propagation
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 311 does not study
   universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study
   propagation consistency within empirical constraints.

6. "propagation strength implies field strength"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that propagation metrics correspond
   to physical field strengths. Phase 311 does not study fields, forces,
   or field theory. We study organizational structure within a mathematical
   model.

7. "consistency implies causal links"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that propagation consistency
   corresponds to causal relationships. Phase 311 does not study causality,
   cause-effect relationships, or physical mechanisms. We study consistency
   preservation under recursive depth.
