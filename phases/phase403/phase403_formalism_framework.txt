# Phase 403 Framework: Cross-Formalism Verification

## Purpose
Determine whether the recursive emergence hierarchy is FORMALISM-INVARIANT across fundamentally different mathematical implementation structures. Distinguish implementation artifact from formalism-independent emergence structure.

## Core Distinction
implementation artifact vs formalism-independent emergence structure

## Formalism Conditions
1. BaselineRecursiveOperator — canonical P-A-N operator system
2. TensorNetworkPropagation — tensor network relational propagation
3. GraphDynamicalRecursion — graph dynamics formalism
4. CategoryMorphismComposition — category/morphism theory
5. GroupoidInteractionFormalism — groupoid interaction structure
6. AlgebraicRecursiveTransformation — algebraic transformation system
7. CellularRelationalPropagation — cellular relational dynamics
8. ProbabilisticRelationalRecursion — probabilistic recursion
9. TopologicalConnectivityRecursion — topological connectivity
10. InformationFlowRecursiveFormalism — information flow dynamics
11. CompressedMinimalFormalism — minimal viable formalism
12. GeneralizedAbstractRelationalFormalism — most abstract representation
13. PartiallyRandomizedFormalism — partially randomized structure
14. HybridMultiFormalismCoupling — combined formalisms
15. NullRandomFormalismControl — complete randomization

## Metrics
- formalism_invariance_score (FIS) — invariance across formalisms
- emergence_translation_fidelity (ETF) — translation faithfulness
- hierarchy_preservation_consistency (HPC) — hierarchy invariance
- cross_formalism_operator_alignment (COA) — operator-role consistency
- universality_retention_index (URI) — universal vs specific structures
- abstraction_equivalence_strength (AES) — abstraction invariance
- implementation_dependency_bound (IDB) — max implementation dependence

## Classification Thresholds
- FORMALISM-STABLE: FIS > 0.80, ETF > 0.80
- FORMALISM-BOUNDED: FIS > 0.60, ETF > 0.60
- FORMALISM-DEGRADING: FIS > 0.40, ETF > 0.40
- FORMALISM-FAILED: FIS <= 0.40
