PHASE 403 FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS

The following interpretations of cross-formalism verification results
are FORBIDDEN. These terms reify mathematical Platonism, metaphysical
universalism, or fundamental physics metaphors as literal properties.
All results must be framed as cross-implementation relational
propagation consistency.

RATIONALIZATION

Phase 403 investigates whether the emergence hierarchy is preserved
across different mathematical implementation structures. The formalisms
tested (tensor networks, graph dynamics, category theory, etc.) are
alternative mathematical representations of the same relational
constraint system — not universal metaphysical truths, fundamental
physics, or Platonic forms. All terminology must remain strictly
relational and comparative across formalisms.

Enforcement: Any use of forbidden terms in phase documentation or
derived publications requires reframing. Repeat violations may
require governance review.
