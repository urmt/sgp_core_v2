SECTION 1: OPERATIONAL DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

A. RELATIONAL CURVATURE
------------------------
Definition: Directional deviation of recursive trajectories
within embedding configuration geometry.

Operationalization:
- Compute cosine of angle between consecutive tangent vectors
- curvature = 1 - cos_angle
- Higher curvature = more directional change

B. CURVATURE FLOW
------------------
Definition: Depth-dependent evolution of relational
trajectory curvature.

Operationalization:
- Track mean_curvature across depths
- Monitor curvature stability (variance)
- Measure flow organization

C. MANIFOLD BENDING
--------------------
Definition: Sector-dependent concentration of recursive
trajectories into curved relational regions.

Operationalization:
- sector_bending = 1 / trajectory_dispersion
- Higher bending = tighter trajectory concentration
- Track bending across depths and sectors

D. CURVATURE STABILITY
-----------------------
Definition: Persistence of curvature organization across
recursive depth.

Operationalization:
- Compute curvature_variance across depths
- Bounded variance = STABLE
- Unbounded variance = UNSTABLE

E. RG-STABLE CURVATURE
------------------------
Definition: Curvature structure preserved under
coarse-graining.

Operationalization:
- Compute fine-scale curvature at multiple depths
- Compare coarse-scale (RG block) curvature
- Correlation > 0.90 = RG_STABLE
