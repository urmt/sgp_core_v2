================================================================================
A. NETWORK COHERENCE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistent large-scale recursive coordination across organizational domains
and interfaces.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which multiple bounded domains and their interfaces collectively
form organized network-scale structures.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coherence strength measures the overall coordination of network organization
across domains and interfaces.

================================================================================
B. MULTI-DOMAIN COORDINATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Stable recursive relational organization spanning multiple bounded domains.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which domain-domain and domain-interface interactions
produce coherent network-scale patterns.

QUANTIFICATION:
Coordination stability measures the persistence of network organization
as recursive depth increases.

================================================================================
C. COHERENCE RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of coordinated network organization across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which network-scale patterns remain identifiable as
recursion increases.

QUANTIFICATION:
Recursive retention measures the persistence of network coherence.

================================================================================
D. BOUNDED NETWORK COUPLING
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded recursive interaction across the network.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which network-scale interactions remain contained rather
than dispersing infinitely.

QUANTIFICATION:
Boundedness measures network-scale containment.
Network coupling measures cross-network interaction strength.

================================================================================
E. NETWORK DISPERSION CONTROL
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Suppression of uncontrolled recursive divergence across organizational
structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which network coherence prevents unbounded divergence
across the network.

QUANTIFICATION:
Dispersion measures the degree of uncontrolled spread across the network.

================================================================================
F. NETWORK RG STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Scale-stable preservation of network coherence.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
The degree to which network coherence survives coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
RG stability measures the correlation between fine and coarse network metrics.
