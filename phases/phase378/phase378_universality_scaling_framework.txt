# Phase 378 Framework: Recursive Universality and Scale-Independent Organization

## Purpose
Determine whether deeply stabilized recursive organizational systems converge toward universal scale-independent organizational behavior.

## Structure

### 1. Scale-Rescaling Invariance
- Apply recursive rescaling transformations
- Measure universality stability (US)
- Identify scale-invariant patterns

### 2. Universality Class Extraction
- Extract universality classes from organizational behavior
- Measure scale-invariant coherence (SIC)
- Identify universal organizational archetypes

### 3. Recursive Renormalization Analysis
- Apply recursive renormalization procedures
- Measure recursive rescaling similarity (RRS)
- Identify renormalization fixed points

### 4. Attractor Topology Scaling
- Track attractor topology under scale transformation
- Measure attractor universality (AU)
- Identify scale-invariant topological forms

### 5. Perturbation Universality Testing
- Apply perturbations across scales
- Measure cross-scale perturbation response
- Identify universal perturbation signatures

### 6. Resilience Scaling Persistence
- Track resilience under scale transformation
- Measure resilience scaling index (RSI)
- Identify scale-independent resilience patterns

### 7. Null Universality Controls
- Randomized universality baselines
- Confirm signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- universality_stability (US)
- scale_invariant_coherence (SIC)
- recursive_rescaling_similarity (RRS)
- attractor_universality (AU)
- resilience_scaling_index (RSI)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- universality_persistence (UP)

## Classification Thresholds
- UNIVERSALITY-STABLE: US > 0.80, SIC > 0.80, RRS > 0.75
- UNIVERSALITY-BOUNDED: US > 0.70, SIC > 0.70, RRS > 0.65
- UNIVERSALITY-PARTIAL: US > 0.60, SIC > 0.60, RRS > 0.55
- UNIVERSALITY-DEGRADING: US > 0.45
- UNIVERSALITY-FAILED: US <= 0.45
