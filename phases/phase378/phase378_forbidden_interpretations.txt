# Phase 378 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Learning, adaptation, memory, recognition
- Meaning, significance, interpretation, representation
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)
- Evolution, emergence (beyond structural pattern description)
- Intelligence, wisdom, knowledge
- Identity, self, personality, character

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Universality as "truth" or "fundamental law"
- Scale-invariance as "timeless" or "eternal"
- Renormalization as "simplification" or "abstraction"
- Attractor universality as "destiny" or "inevitability"
- Perturbation universality as "universal vulnerability"
- Resilience scaling as "universal strength"
- Scale-independent organization as "perfect design"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric, topological, algebraic
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 378 analyzes recursive universality and scale-independent organization as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
