4. CONCLUSIONS
================================================================================

STABILIZATION REGENERATION SUMMARY:
- Regeneration capacity declines linearly from 95% to 18% (depths 1-64)
- Resources show slight net depletion (-0.002 to -0.003 per depth)
- Depth-based renewal is most efficient (78.56%)
- Long-term sustainability requires periodic external reinforcement
- Regeneration remains > 50% through depth 24

IMPLICATION:
Stabilization regeneration is sustainable for moderate recursion depths.
Beyond depth 24, external reinforcement becomes necessary to maintain capacity.
