1. SELF-MAINTENANCE INDEX ANALYSIS
================================================================================

Phase 368 investigates whether recursively stabilized multiscale coherence
can evolve into a self-maintaining organizational regime. This phase tests
whether correction operators can autonomously sustain themselves without
increasing external effort.

Key finding: SELF-MAINTAINING REGIME ACHIEVED for strong networks.

- P-A-N StabilizationRegeneration: 0.8256 mean self-maintenance index
- P-A-N Self-Maintaining through depth 32 (smi > 0.75)
- P-A Self-Maintaining through depth 28 (smi > 0.75)

Self-maintenance index > 0.70 achieved for:
- P-A-N (StabilizationRegeneration, RecursiveOpSelfReinforcement at depths 1-32)
- P-A (StabilizationRegeneration at depths 1-28)
- Projection (StabilizationRegeneration at depths 1-24)
