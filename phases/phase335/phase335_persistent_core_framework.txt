================================================================================
A. PERSISTENT ORGANIZATIONAL CORE
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Organizational region that exhibits unusually stable properties
across recursive depth, resisting degradation more effectively
than surrounding organizational structure.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Core persistence measures the degree to which organizational regions
retain structural integrity through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Persistence = structural_retention_across_depths / max_retention

================================================================================
B. CORE BOUNDEDNESS
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Constraint on unbounded divergence of core structural properties
during recursive evolution.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Core boundedness quantifies the degree to which core deformation
remains controlled rather than drifting unboundedly.

QUANTIFICATION:
Boundedness = 1 - (drift_variance / max_variance)

================================================================================
C. CORE CONCENTRATION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Measure of how organizational structure increasingly concentrates
into stable persistent regions versus spreading diffusely.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Concentration ratio quantifies the degree to which organizational
properties become concentrated in core regions.

QUANTIFICATION:
Concentration = core_strength / total_organization

================================================================================
D. RECURSIVE CORE STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of core structure under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Recursive stability measures the degree to which core properties
survive coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability = correlation(fine_core, coarse_core)

================================================================================
E. SECTOR PERSISTENCE HIERARCHY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Stable ordering of sector persistence across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Hierarchy preservation measures the consistency of sector ordering
through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Hierarchy = ordering_preservation(depths) / max_depths
