4. "mind geometry confirmed"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that transport-embedding compatibility
   corresponds to mental geometry, cognitive structure, or mind-space.
   Phase 334 does not study mind or cognitive structure.

5. "semantic transport discovered"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that transport-embedding coordination
   corresponds to semantic meaning, linguistic content, or information
   transport. Phase 334 does not study semantics or language.

6. "physics substrate identified"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that compatibility structure confirms
   physical substrate, spacetime geometry, or material existence.
   Phase 334 does not study physical reality or materiality.

7. "bounded interference proves consciousness coupling"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that bounded interference between
   transport and embedding corresponds to consciousness coupling, experiential
   binding, or unified awareness. Phase 334 does not study consciousness.
