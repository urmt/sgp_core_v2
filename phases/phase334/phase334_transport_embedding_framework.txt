================================================================================
A. TRANSPORT-EMBEDDING COMPATIBILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Degree to which transport organization preserves embedding organization,
measured as mutual organizational coherence between transport and
embedding structures.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Compatibility strength quantifies the degree to which transport
organization maintains alignment with embedding organization.

QUANTIFICATION:
Compatibility = mutual_coherence(transport, embedding) / max_coherence

================================================================================
B. INTERFERENCE DRIFT
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Magnitude of organizational disruption between transport and
embedding structures when they interact.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Interference drift quantifies the degree of organizational
displacement due to transport-embedding interaction.

QUANTIFICATION:
Interference = displacement_magnitude / baseline_organization

================================================================================
C. CROSS-DEPTH COMPATIBILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of transport-embedding compatibility across recursive depth.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Cross-depth compatibility measures the preservation of mutual
organization coherence through recursive evolution.

QUANTIFICATION:
Compatibility = correlation(compatibility_across_depths)

================================================================================
D. COMPATIBILITY RETENTION
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Fraction of transport and embedding structures maintaining
mutual organizational coherence.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Retention coefficient quantifies the fraction of compatible
structures surviving transformation.

QUANTIFICATION:
Retention = compatible_structures / total_structures

================================================================================
E. RECURSIVE COMPATIBILITY STABILITY
================================================================================

DEFINITION:
Persistence of compatible transport-embedding organization
under recursive transformation.

OPERATIONAL MEASURE:
Stability measures the degree to which compatibility survives
coarse-graining operations.

QUANTIFICATION:
Stability = 1 - variance(compatibility) / max_variance

================================================================================
TRANSPORT-EMBEDDING COMPATIBILITY FRAMEWORK FORMALIZATION
================================================================================

TE_STRUCTURE = (C_str, I_drift, C_comp, C_ret, RG_sim)

Where:
- C_str: Compatibility strength
- I_drift: Interference drift
- C_comp: Cross-depth compatibility
- C_ret: Compatibility retention
