# Phase 397 Framework: Recursive Localization and Particle-Like Persistence Regimes

## Purpose
Determine whether recursive wave/interference organizational systems naturally generate stable localized persistence structures from constrained relational propagation dynamics alone.

## Core Distinction
distributed recursive wave organization vs stable localized recursive persistence structures

## Structure

### 1. Localized Packet Emergence
- Extract localized packet-like structures from wave organization
- Measure localization coherence (LC)
- Identify packet-stable regimes

### 2. Recursive Localization Persistence Tracking
- Track persistence of localized structures across recursion
- Measure packet persistence (PP)
- Identify localization degradation patterns

### 3. Constructive Interference Confinement Analysis
- Analyze confinement from constructive interference patterns
- Measure interference confinement strength (ICS)
- Identify confinement-stable regimes

### 4. Bounded Propagation Coherence Mapping
- Map coherence of bounded propagation structures
- Measure bounded transport stability (BTS)
- Identify bounded propagation regimes

### 5. Recursive Identity-Preserving Transport Analysis
- Track identity retention during recursive transport
- Measure recursive identity retention (RIR)
- Identify identity-stable regimes

### 6. Localization Stability Under Perturbation
- Analyze localization stability under perturbation
- Measure localization gain (LG)
- Identify stabilization thresholds

### 7. Null Localization Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm localization signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- localization_coherence (LC)
- packet_persistence (PP)
- bounded_transport_stability (BTS)
- recursive_identity_retention (RIR)
- interference_confinement_strength (ICS)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- localization_gain (LG)

## Classification Thresholds
- LOCALIZATION-STABLE: LC > 0.90, PP > 0.85, BTS > 0.85
- LOCALIZATION-BOUNDED: LC > 0.80, PP > 0.75, BTS > 0.75
- LOCALIZATION-PARTIAL: LC > 0.70, PP > 0.65, BTS > 0.65
- LOCALIZATION-DEGRADING: LC > 0.55
- LOCALIZATION-FAILED: LC <= 0.55
