RATIONALIZATION

Phase 401 investigates whether emergent relational organization survives
perturbation. The perturbations examined (additive noise, jitter,
disruption, corruption, scrambling, memory loss) are mathematical
distortions of recursive constraint propagation — not biological
stress responses, engineering fault tolerance, or quantum decoherence.
All terminology must remain strictly relational and mathematical.
