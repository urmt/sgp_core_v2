# Phase 396 Framework: Recursive Wave-Mode Organization and Interference Dynamics

## Purpose
Determine whether quantized recursive stabilization regimes naturally generate wave-like propagation modes and interference organization from recursive relational dynamics alone.

## Core Distinction
quantized recursive stabilization vs emergent recursive wave/interference organization

## Structure

### 1. Wave-Mode Extraction
- Extract stable wave-like propagation modes
- Measure wave coherence (WC)
- Identify mode-stable regimes

### 2. Recursive Interference Coherence Analysis
- Analyze interference coherence patterns across recursion
- Measure interference stability (IS)
- Identify interference-stable regimes

### 3. Standing-Mode Persistence Tracking
- Track persistence of standing-mode structures
- Measure standing mode persistence (SMP)
- Identify standing-mode degradation patterns

### 4. Oscillatory Transport Stability Mapping
- Map stability of oscillatory transport behavior
- Measure oscillatory transport alignment (OTA)
- Identify transport-stable regimes

### 5. Constructive/Destructive Interaction Analysis
- Analyze constructive and destructive relational patterns
- Measure interference gain (IG)
- Identify interaction regimes

### 6. Recursive Mode Synchronization
- Track synchronization of recursive modes
- Measure recursive mode synchronization (RMS)
- Identify synchronization thresholds

### 7. Null Wave Controls
- Randomized relational baselines
- Confirm wave/interference signal is not artifact
- Establish significance thresholds

## Metrics
- wave_coherence (WC)
- interference_stability (IS)
- standing_mode_persistence (SMP)
- oscillatory_transport_alignment (OTA)
- recursive_mode_synchronization (RMS)
- rg_similarity (RGS)
- interference_gain (IG)

## Classification Thresholds
- WAVE-STABLE: WC > 0.90, IS > 0.85, SMP > 0.85
- WAVE-BOUNDED: WC > 0.80, IS > 0.75, SMP > 0.75
- WAVE-PARTIAL: WC > 0.70, IS > 0.65, SMP > 0.65
- WAVE-DEGRADING: WC > 0.55
- WAVE-FAILED: WC <= 0.55
