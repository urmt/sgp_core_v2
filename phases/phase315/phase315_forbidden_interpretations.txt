# PHASE 315: FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
# Emergent Relational Differentiation Geometry

================================================================================
STRICT INTERPRETATION CONSTRAINTS
================================================================================

This document explicitly prohibits certain interpretations of Phase 315
results. These interpretations would violate the empirical and mathematical
constraints established in prior phases.

================================================================================
FORBIDDEN INTERPRETATIONS
================================================================================

The following interpretations are explicitly prohibited:

1. "differentiation proves individuality"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that differentiated structure implies
   individual existence or separateness. Phase 315 does not study individuality,
   personal identity, or singular existence. We study organizational
   differentiation within a mathematical framework.

2. "recursive differentiation proves agency"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that recursive differentiation
   corresponds to agency or goal-directed behavior. Phase 315 does not
   study agency, intention, or purposive action. We study persistence and
   stability within a mathematical model.

3. "stable differentiation proves intelligence"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that stable differentiated organization
   implies intelligent behavior or cognitive capacity. Phase 315 does not
   address questions of intelligence, cognition, or mental processes. We study
   organizational differentiation within empirical constraints.

4. "RG differentiation proves cognition"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that renormalization group
   differentiation stability implies cognitive processes. Phase 315 does not
   study cognition, awareness, or mental states. We study scale invariance
   within a mathematical framework.

5. "organizational differentiation is universe generation"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that differentiated structure
   corresponds to universe or cosmos generation. Phase 315 does not study
   universe formation, cosmological evolution, or genesis. We study
   bounded differentiation within empirical constraints.

6. "differentiation strength implies biological specialization"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that differentiation metrics correspond
   to biological specialization or cell differentiation. Phase 315 does not
   study biological systems, organisms, or developmental processes. We study
   organizational structure within a mathematical model.

7. "divergence stability implies evolutionary fitness"

   REASON: This interpretation assumes that divergence stability corresponds
   to evolutionary fitness or natural selection. Phase 315 does not study
   evolution, fitness landscapes, or adaptive processes. We study structural
   distinguishability under recursive evolution.
