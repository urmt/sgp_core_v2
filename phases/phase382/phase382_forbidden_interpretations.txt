# Phase 382 Forbidden Interpretations

## PROHIBITED LANGUAGE
- Consciousness, awareness, cognition, perception, understanding
- Intentionality, purpose, goal-directed, designed
- Learning, adaptation, memory, recognition
- Meaning, significance, interpretation, representation
- Agency, autonomy, self-organization (beyond structural description)
- Evolution, emergence (beyond structural pattern description)
- Intelligence, wisdom, knowledge
- Identity, self, personality, character

## PROHIBITED CONCEPTS
- Emergent modes as "entities" or "beings"
- Mode persistence as "survival" or "life"
- Independent transport as "autonomy" or "freedom"
- Mode stabilization as "self-awareness" or "self-determination"
- Composite mode interaction as "social behavior" or "community"
- Mode reproduction as "reproduction" or "procreation"
- Emergent organizational modes as "new forms of consciousness"

## REQUIRED LANGUAGE
- Structural, geometric, topological, algebraic
- Relational, organizational, pattern-based
- Metric-driven, threshold-based, classification-based
- Depth-dependent, operator-dependent, condition-dependent
- Quantitative, empirical, computational

## CORE PRINCIPLE
Phase 382 analyzes emergent organizational mode formation as structural patterns in recursive computational systems. All descriptions must remain strictly mathematical and geometric.
