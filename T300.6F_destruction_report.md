## Part 3: Final defensible claims

After adversarial destruction, the following claims can be defended:

1. **μ is weakly constrained (T1/T2 only)** — the ratio m_p/m_e has a
   fundamental lower bound from chemistry; no claims of improbability
   beyond the bound itself

2. **Λ has an anthropic upper bound** — Λ must be <~10⁻¹¹⁶ to permit
   galaxy formation; the Garriga-Vilenkin prediction is successful;
   interpreted as evidence for multiverse rather than fine-tuning

3. **Depends on definition of life** — if you assume carbon-only
   complex life, α and αs show narrow bounds from the Hoyle state
   (but both are single unreplicated papers from the 1980s)

   and αs ±0.5%)
